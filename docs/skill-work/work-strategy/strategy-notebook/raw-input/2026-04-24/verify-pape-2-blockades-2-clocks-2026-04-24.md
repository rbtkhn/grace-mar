## Interpretations (non-falsifiable in one pass)

- **“Fewer than 5 percent”** in the Substack maps to Pape’s **115-case HSE slice** and his **5** residual successes — **not** to the **expanded third-edition** observation count. For notebook prose, either **cite Pape 1997 explicitly** or **update denominator** if you import newer HSE statistics.
- **“Significant shocks”** for Russia mixes **early-2022 forecast drama** (e.g. IMF −6% [Jul 2022 Interfax](https://interfax.com/newsroom/top-stories/81648/), WB −11.2% [press coverage](https://m.akipress.com/news:669941:Russia_s_GDP_is_projected_to_go_down_by_11_2_percent_in_2022_-_World_Bank/)) with **realized** **−2.1%** (World Bank outturn). **Do not merge forecast and outturn** without a tier tag.
- **Iraq “50%”**: credible for **real production / early sanctions decade**; **dollar GDP per capita** also collapsed via **FX** — decompose if a cite must be **forensic**.
