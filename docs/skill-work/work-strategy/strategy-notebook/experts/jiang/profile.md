## Signature mechanisms

- **Civilizational game-theory** — state behavior as iterated strategic interaction across civilizational time scales
- **Petrodollar / financial architecture** — monetary and energy systems as structural constraints on geopolitics
- **Historical pattern extraction** — recurring civilizational dynamics (rise, overextension, adaptation) as predictive templates

## Recurrent claims

- Long-arc civilizational patterns are more predictive than short-cycle geopolitical analysis
- Financial architecture (petrodollar, reserve currency, sanctions regime) is a structural constraint that shapes military and diplomatic options
- Game-theory reasoning applied to civilizational interactions produces reusable mechanisms, not just historical narrative
