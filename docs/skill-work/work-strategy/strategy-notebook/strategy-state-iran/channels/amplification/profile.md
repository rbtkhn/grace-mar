# Channel — amplification (republication / repost / echo)
<!-- word_count: 209 -->

**Formal role:** **Non-primary** **paths** the signal takes — **Tasnim**-class **republication**, **X** **repost** chains, **influential** **quote-boosts** of a **Majlis** or **press** line — **the chain is part of the judgment object** (provenance, not “noise” to strip).

## High-value evidence for

- **How far** a line **traveled**, **which** **elite** **nodes** **endorsed** it, **domestic** **coordination** **hints** (same **phrase** in **two** **registers** same day).

## Weak evidence for (without primary)

- **Truth** of the **underlying** **claim** — always **stabilize** on **MFA** / **wire** / **field** as appropriate.

## Usual register

- **Headline** **echo** or **full** **repost**; **fa** in **OEM** with **EN** **gloss** in **raw-input** tables.

## Usual audience

- **Domestic** **mass** or **insider** **Twitter**; **not** a **substitute** for **institutional** **primary** when the **seam** is **policy** **text**.

## Common lag

- **Near-real-time**; can **precede** **English** **FM** by **hours** or **lag** a **day**.

## Seam priority (into main `days.md`)

- **Tag** **repost** **depth** and **node** in [main `chapters/…/days.md`](../../chapters/2026-04/days.md) when the **analyst** **arc** is **narrative** **spread**, not **FM** **substance** **alone**.

## Contradiction risks

- **Repost** **headline** **vs** **long-form** **FM** readout; **Marandi** **boost** of **Majlis** **line** **vs** **Marandi** **own** **voice** in [`experts/`](../../experts/) — **seam** `thread:marandi` with **register** care.
