# Channel — diplomacy (FM / foreign ministry register)

**Formal role:** IRI **foreign ministry** and **head-of-mission** English (and other) **official** lines — X accounts, readouts, press-adjacent material labeled MFA.

## High-value evidence for

- **Announced** negotiation posture, **ceasefire** / **de-escalation** **terms of art**, **third-party** tracks (e.g. Islamabad), **multilateral** talking points the ministry owns.

## Weak evidence for (without corroboration)

- **Material** strait state, **ORBAT**, **kinetic** fact, **on-water** commercial passage — use [material-check](../material-check/profile.md) and wire/AIS, not FM alone.

## Usual register

- Careful **English** comms, **IHL** / **civilian** frames, **status URLs** on posts when available.

## Usual audience (implicit)

- International press, capitals, **domestic** English-literate readership; sometimes **pair** with `fa` lines elsewhere.

## Common lag

- **Hours to days** behind kinetic reality; can **lead** narrative before **field** readouts.

## Seam priority (into main `days.md`)

- **First** for **“what the ministry wants read”**; **seam** with [main chapter `days.md`](../../chapters/2026-04/days.md) as **announced** lane; **mark** when judgment needs **Majlis** or **U.S. commentator** same-week compare.

## Contradiction risks

- **FM open-for-commerce** / **Hormuz** phrasing **vs** [material-check](material-check/profile.md) / AIS; **MFA** line **vs** **Majlis** coercion vocabulary same week — **do not** merge without **signal-class** and **tier** tags.
