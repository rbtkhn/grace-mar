## Media coverage: Algiers (2026) vs earlier papal mosque visits (pattern sketch)

**Scope:** Editorial lines differ by **outlet**, **language**, and **country**. Below is **pattern-level** comparison for **English-language** and **wire**-shaped coverage — **not** a content audit of every article.

**What repeats (stable “mosque story” grammar):** **Historic** / **symbolic** ledes; **interfaith** **dialogue** and **fraternity**; **shoes**, **silence**, **rector** / **mufti**; **sacred** **space**; **Holy See**-friendly wires (**Reuters**, **BBC**, **Vatican News**) foregrounding **tone** and **schedule**. The **core** **shape**—**pope** **+** **mosque** **=** **gesture** **of** **respect**—is **largely** **stable** across **Damascus** **(2001)**, **Istanbul** **(2006** / **2014)**, and **Algiers** **(2026)**.

**What differed by era (the “second story”):**

- **John Paul II — Damascus (2001):** **Novelty** **dominated**: **first** **pope** **inside** **a** **mosque**, **Jubilee** / **Pauline** **frame**, **forgiveness** **language**. **Political** **noise** often tracked **host-state** **and** **regional** **spin**, **not** a **sitting** **U.S.** **president** **vs** **pope** **subplot**.
- **Benedict XVI — Istanbul (2006):** **Regensburg** **aftermath** — **repair**, **risk**, **some** **intra-Catholic** **and** **Muslim** **skepticism**; coverage often **split** **damage-control** **from** **pure** **theology**.
- **Francis — Istanbul (2014):** **Regional** **war** **backdrop** (**Syria** / **Iraq**, **IS**); **Turkey** as **border** **state**; **multi-stop** **tour** (**Hagia Sophia**, **Orthodox** **echelons**). **No** **Trump** **presidency** — the **U.S.** **angle** was **not** **“executive** **vs** **pope”** **in** **the** **2026** **sense**.
- **Leo XIV — Algiers (2026):** Same **interfaith** **shell**, **plus**: **first** **papal** **visit** **to** **Algeria**; **Africa** **itinerary** / **periphery**; **superlative** **building** (**scale**, **minaret**) as **easy** **visual** **lede**; **Augustine** / **“land** **of** **my** **spiritual** **father”** as a **distinct** **Leo** **hook**. **English** **coverage** **often** **layers** **U.S.–Vatican** **tension** (**executive** **attacks**, **Iran** **war** **context**) **and** **meta** **on** **“politician** **vs** **pope”** (e.g. **plane** **lines** **in** **Vatican** **copy**), so **mosque** **imagery** is **read** **through** **Washington–Holy** **See** **friction** **as** **much** **as** **through** **pure** **Christian–Muslim** **analysis**.

**One-line takeaway:** **Earlier** **mosque** **trips** were **framed** **chiefly** **as** **firsts**, **repairs**, **or** **regional-war** **context**; **Algiers** **2026** **often** **adds** **a** **thick** **American** **political** **and** **Middle** **East** **war** **overlay** **on** **top** **of** **the** **same** **interfaith** **template**.
