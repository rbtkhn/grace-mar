# WAP outreach workspace

Canonical operator entrypoint for outreach inside `work-american-politics`.

Use this file when you want to answer:

- what offer is being tested
- who it is for
- what proof supports it
- how outreach outcomes are being tracked
- what objections are teaching us

---

## What outreach is for

Outreach here is a market-learning surface, not a mass-email engine.

Use it to learn:

- which offer framing creates curiosity
- which buyer segment actually cares
- what proof builds trust
- where friction appears in the funnel

---

## Canonical files

| File | Role |
|------|------|
| `offers.md` | Current offer framing and bounded entry offers |
| `proof-ledger.md` | Reusable proof fragments and operational outcomes |
| `target-registry.md` | Narrow target segments and lead-source logic |
| `outreach-funnel.md` | Stage-by-stage outreach tracking |
| `objection-log.md` | Structured learning from replies and meetings |
| `workspace.md` | Broader WAP operator workspace for campaign operations |

---

## Operator path

1. Start in `offers.md` and choose one offer to test.
2. Confirm the matching segment in `target-registry.md`.
3. Pull one or two proof lines from `proof-ledger.md`.
4. Run a small outreach batch.
5. Log outcomes in `outreach-funnel.md`.
6. Log pushback and what it means in `objection-log.md`.
7. Adjust offer, proof, or segment before the next batch.

---

## Weekly rhythm

| Step | Question |
|------|----------|
| **Offer check** | Are we still leading with operator outcomes instead of tooling? |
| **Segment check** | Are we talking to the most plausible buyer for this offer? |
| **Proof check** | Do we have one credible line that reduces trust friction? |
| **Funnel check** | Where is the biggest drop-off this week? |
| **Objection check** | What did replies teach us about fit, trust, timing, or scope? |

---

## Guardrails

- Keep outreach bounded, truthful, and companion-led.
- Do not overclaim technical maturity.
- Do not frame WAP primarily as "AI automation."
- Political trust and discretion matter more than clever outbound tricks.
