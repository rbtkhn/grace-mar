# WAP offers

Canonical offer surface for `work-american-politics`.

Use this file to keep outreach outcome-first:

- lead with campaign usefulness, not tooling
- keep the first ask low-risk
- make promises concrete and bounded
- do not claim maturity or scale beyond what WAP can actually deliver

---

## Current offers

| Offer | Audience | Problem | Outcome | Timeframe | Entry ask | Delivery shape | Notes |
|------|----------|---------|---------|-----------|-----------|----------------|-------|
| **Campaign briefing diagnostic** | Small campaign operators; political consultants; issue operators | Briefing, content, and operator visibility are fragmented or reactive | One clear memo showing bottlenecks, dropped follow-ups, stale inputs, and the next workflow surface to fix first | 3-5 business days | Share current briefing / content rhythm and one active need | One memo plus one proposed working surface | Primary low-risk offer. Best first test because it is easy to understand and easy to scope. |
| **Weekly message ops support** | Campaigns or operators with an active weekly rhythm | Weekly brief, source triage, and content prep take too much manual coordination | A repeatable weekly cycle: source readiness, brief scaffold, content queue, blockers, and next actions | Weekly cadence | Review one sample brief cycle together | Weekly brief scaffold + queue review + blocker summary | Best after trust exists or after a diagnostic. |
| **Content queue and briefing setup** | Teams with inconsistent post workflow or no clear prep path | Ideas, drafts, and review state live in chat threads or memory | One structured queue and one brief-prep path that makes the next cycle easier to run | 2-4 business days | Show current content process and target posting rhythm | Queue setup + source list + operator notes | Good bridge offer when the problem is workflow clarity, not full weekly support. |

---

## Default outreach framing

| Layer | Guidance |
|------|----------|
| **Lead with** | Faster briefing, cleaner message operations, fewer dropped follow-ups, clearer operator visibility |
| **Do not lead with** | "AI automations," dashboards, agents, or workflow internals |
| **First ask** | Offer a bounded diagnostic or sample surface, not an open-ended build |
| **Proof style** | Use concrete operational changes from [proof-ledger.md](proof-ledger.md), not abstract capability claims |
