## Tactics and strategies (summary)

### Against establishment incumbents
- **Frame:** Washington swamp, lobbyist capture, career politician.
- **Contrast:** Principle vs party line; constitutional vs expedient.
- **Evidence:** Vote records, omnibus support, foreign aid, Ukraine.

### Against hawk / interventionist opponents
- **Frame:** Endless wars; Congress must decide; $8T Middle East.
- **Contrast:** Massie/Khanna war-powers posture vs opponent.
- **Evidence:** War-powers votes, Iran, Israel aid.

### Against Trump-endorsed opponents (when Massie supports challenger)
- **Frame:** Principle over endorsement; constitutional restraint is America First.
- **Contrast:** Rand Paul, Jordan, Gaetz, MTG endorse Massie-type candidates.
- **Careful:** Don't attack Trump; reframe vocabulary.

### Religious / spiritual profile methodology
- **Include when:** Candidate publicly states church affiliation, faith, pastoral role, or faith-based messaging.
- **"Not publicly disclosed" when:** No documented religious statement found.
