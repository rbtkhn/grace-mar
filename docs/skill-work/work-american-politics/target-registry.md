# WAP target registry

Canonical target-segment surface for `work-american-politics`.

Use this file to decide who outreach is for before building lists or writing copy.

Start narrow:

- choose one segment at a time
- note why the segment fits
- record what pain seems most likely
- only then gather actual leads

---

## Target segments

| Segment | Likely buyer | Why fit | Likely pain points | Lead-source ideas | Quality notes | Status |
|--------|--------------|---------|--------------------|-------------------|---------------|--------|
| **Small campaign operators** | Campaign manager; principal; chief of staff | Need useful briefing and message support without adding a large team | Fragmented weekly prep, reactive research, dropped follow-ups, unclear next actions | Candidate sites, local campaign ecosystems, state party ecosystems, campaign vendor adjacency | Strong fit for the diagnostic offer; trust-sensitive and likely bandwidth-constrained | **active_test** |
| **Boutique political consultants** | Consultant owner; account lead | Handle multiple principals and often need more operator visibility or repeatable prep | Too much context switching, inconsistent briefing, uneven content prep across clients | Consultant rosters, political vendor directories, referral chains, issue/political conferences | Attractive because one workflow may generalize across multiple accounts | **researching** |
| **Issue-advocacy or media operators** | Communications lead; research lead; founder | Need structured source triage and message rhythm around one issue area | Ad hoc briefing, no clean queue, narrative drift, weak handoff between research and messaging | Advocacy org ecosystems, issue newsletters, regional media/policy operators | Good fit when the need is message operations, not full campaign strategy | **researching** |

---

## Segment selection rule

Pick the next segment based on:

1. strongest fit with the current offer
2. easiest path to a trustworthy conversation
3. clearest operational pain, not the largest market size
