# WAP outreach funnel

Lightweight funnel tracker for `work-american-politics`.

Use this file to learn by stage:

- who was identified
- who was contacted
- who replied
- who looked like a fit
- what happened next

Do not overcomplicate this. A clean small log is better than a fake CRM.

---

## Status vocabulary

Use one of:

- `identified`
- `contacted`
- `replied`
- `positive_reply`
- `meeting_booked`
- `pilot_proposed`
- `won`
- `lost`
- `deferred`

## What to learn from this file

Ask these questions each week:

1. Which segment gets the strongest positive signal?
2. Which offer creates curiosity without too much friction?
3. Which lane performs better for this offer: direct or partner-led?
4. Where does the funnel break most often: no reply, weak interest, no meeting, or no close?

---

## Guardrail

Track what happened plainly. Do not inflate reply quality or fit just to make the funnel look good.
