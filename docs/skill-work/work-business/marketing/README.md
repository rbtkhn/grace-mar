## Shared principles

1. **Track spend in the ledger.** Marketing expenses go through `emit_business_transaction.py --category advertising` or `--category marketing`. This feeds the P&L and lets you see marketing ROI by venture.
2. **Seasonal calendar.** Major retail windows (Valentine's, Mother's Day, holiday) should appear in venture marketing plans with prep timelines.
3. **Channel-specific.** Each channel (Etsy SEO, social, email, paid) gets its own section in the venture plan with measurable targets.
4. **Evidence-linked.** Campaign results (impressions, clicks, conversion, revenue attributed) logged as notes in the venture marketing plan or as ledger entries with receipt references.
