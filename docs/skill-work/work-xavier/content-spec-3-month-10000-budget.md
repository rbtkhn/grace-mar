**Account:** `@usa_first_ky`  
**Surface:** Unofficial analysis and message support  
**Campaign window:** 12 weeks (KY-4 primary ramp)  
**Budget cap:** **$10,000 total** (hard cap)  
**Doctrine:** Human approves ship; evidence-first; stress-test high-stakes; no autonomous posting

---

## 1) Objective and constraints

### Primary objective
Increase persuasive reach and conversion signals for KY-4 primary support while preserving handbook doctrine:
- principle-first voice
- source discipline
- adversary restraint

## 5) Quality gates and stress-test requirements

### Always required before posting
- Source attached for every factual claim
- Voice checklist pass:
  - declarative
  - principle-first
  - no official impersonation
  - no personal attack framing
- Unofficial label intact where needed

### High-stakes posts (mandatory stress-test)
Triggers:
- war powers escalation context
- transparency/Epstein narratives
- adversary-counter narratives in active attack cycles

Failure modes:
1. Tail risk / stale context
2. Reasoning-output mismatch
3. Social anchoring pressure
4. Vibe drift over evidence

Decision outcomes:
- **PASS:** may ship after human review
- **HOLD:** escalate to companion (timing/content fix)
- **FAIL:** do not ship

---

## 6) KPI specification

### Core KPIs (weekly)
- Output adherence: `% of planned posts shipped`
- Source integrity: `% posts with complete receipts`
- Engagement depth:
  - thread completion proxy (replies/bookmarks)
  - repost ratio
  - high-signal comment count
- Segment coverage: count by target segment
- Risk compliance:
  - HOLD count
  - FAIL count
  - escalations resolved

### Primary-window KPI (Month 3 emphasis)
- Registration CTA interaction trend (clicks/replies if available)
- KY-4 local post reach trend
- Week-over-week lift on best two pillars

---

## 7) Decision rules and escalation

Escalate before ship when:
- factual uncertainty remains
- stress-test is HOLD/FAIL
- live attack/ad drop changes context materially
- legal/media/harassment signal appears
- draft may be interpreted as official campaign office statement

No escalation bypass is allowed for time pressure.

---

## 8) Budget guardrails (non-negotiable)

- No category may exceed allocation by >10% without companion approval
- Contingency use must be logged with reason + linked post IDs
- If cumulative spend reaches **85%** before week 11:
  - freeze experiments
  - prioritize proven formats only
  - reserve remaining budget for registration + final recap/GOTV
