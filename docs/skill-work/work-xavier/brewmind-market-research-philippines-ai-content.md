## 1. Top-down narrative (policy + jobs)

- **National framing:** The **National AI Strategy Roadmap 2.0 (NAISR 2.0)** (DTI-led) positions AI around competitiveness, public services, governance, R&D, and **workforce upskilling**. See e.g. [eLegal.ph — DTI unveils NAISR 2.0](https://elegal.ph/dti-unveils-national-ai-strategy-roadmap-2-0-center-for-ai-research/) and [Newsbytes — NEDA, DTI vow to implement roadmap](https://newsbytes.ph/2024/07/05/neda-dti-vow-to-implement-national-ai-strategy-roadmap-2-0/).
- **Education:** DepEd’s **Project AGAP.AI** and related **AI in basic education** initiatives ([DepEd — Future-Ready EducaItion launch](https://www.deped.gov.ph/2026/01/09/future-ready-educaition-pbbm-angara-naglunsad-ng-ai-program-para-suportahan-ang-mga-guro-at-mag-aaral/), [Daily Tribune — DepEd rolls out Project AGAP.AI](https://tribune.net.ph/2026/01/09/deped-rolls-out-project-agapai)) plus **foundational guidelines** ([DepEd — DO 003, s. 2026 AI in basic education](https://www.deped.gov.ph/2026/02/20/february-20-2026-do-003-s-2026-foundational-guidelines-on-artificial-intelligence-ai-in-basic-education/)) keep **“AI at school”** in mainstream discourse — often **ethics, safety, policy**, not tool tutorials.
- **Workforce / BPO:** Business and trade press link AI to **upskilling and displacement risk**, e.g. [Nearshore Americas — AI pivot / upskilling](https://nearshoreamericas.com/the-big-ai-pivot-upskilling-is-looked-at-as-savior-for-philippines/), [Outsource Accelerator — digital upskilling](https://news.outsourceaccelerator.com/philippines-boosts-digital-upskilling/), [Philstar — Marcos OKs BPO upskilling funding](https://www.philstar.com/headlines/2025/04/27/2438658/marcos-oks-funding-upskilling-bpo-workers/). Industry angles such as [IBPAP / early AI adoption](https://news.outsourceaccelerator.com/philippine-bpos-early-ai-adoption/) reinforce **“AI at work”** as normal.

**Content implication:** Default **public story** = national competitiveness + schools + BPO + ethics. **English** heavy in official and BPO layers; **Filipino / bilingual** in DepEd and mass-media summaries.

## 2. News and general internet

- Tech and broadsheet coverage often frames AI through **viral culture and rights**: e.g. [Manila Bulletin — viral track / AI debate](https://mb.com.ph/2026/03/30/viral-hit-hawak-mo-ang-beat-ignites-ai-debate), [Inquirer Tech — voice / AI influencers](https://technology.inquirer.net/145658/mona-gonzales-ai-voice-issue).
- **Positioning hook:** BrewMind can **contrast** “creepy / fake AI” narratives with **transparent, in-venue, human-staffed** learning and **approved** information.

---

## 3. Social and creator economy

- **AI-generated influencers** and brand tie-ins appear in local listicles, e.g. [8List — Filipina AI influencer](https://8list.ph/lina-lately-first-filipina-ai-influencer/).
- **Volume AI content monetization** (international reporting on PH-based channels): e.g. [Primetimer — AI slop / YouTube](https://www.primetimer.com/news/filipino-youtuber-reveals-how-he-generates-thousands-of-dollars-by-creating-ai-slop).
- **Contrast:** Authentic human creators remain the trust anchor for food/lifestyle (reference pattern; not a BrewMind competitor list).

---

## 4. Paid / structured learning market

- **Affordable / Filipino-facing funnels:** e.g. [The ChatGPT Blueprint](https://www.pinoychatgptblueprint.com/) — freelancers/sellers, practical ChatGPT + earning angle, Filipino marketing.
- **Corporate / classroom vendors (English, higher ticket):** e.g. [NobleProg — ChatGPT training Philippines](https://www.nobleprog.com.ph/chatgpt-training) ([beginner course incl. Cebu](https://www.nobleprog.com.ph/cc/chatgptbegin)), [Nexacu Philippines — ChatGPT beginner](http://nexacu.com/ph/ai-for-business/chatgpt-beginner/).

**Implication:** Online landscape skews **barbell** — cheap “hustle” content and **expensive** corporate training. **Middle** = short, trusted, **physical** touchpoints (partner café + QR + micro-lessons).

## 5. Language and format

| Layer | Typical language | Typical format |
|-------|------------------|----------------|
| Government / BPO / premium training | English | Articles, PDFs, long courses |
| DepEd / mass social | Filipino / Taglish | Video, shares, comments |
| Creator “earn with AI” | Mixed | Short video, landing pages |

**Under-saturated positioning:** “**5-minute lesson in a real venue**” as a **named category** — own via **offline + QR + partner density** (Cebu pilot).

## 6. Strategic gaps BrewMind can claim

| Crowded online | Relatively open |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Generic ChatGPT “make money” funnels | **In-person habit** + **neighborhood density** |
| AI influencer / deepfake anxiety | **Human venues**, transparent staff, partner brands |
| Long bootcamps / pricey corporate classes | **Micro-lessons**, low entry (aligned with [BrewMind site](https://xavier-x01.github.io/brew_mind/)) |
| English-only pro training | **Bilingual** microcopy and staff scripts where useful |
