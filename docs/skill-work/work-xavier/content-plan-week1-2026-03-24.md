## 2. Voice and strategy (applied)

- Voice: Direct, declarative, principle-first. 'X won't Y.' No 'Mr. President.' Evidence-based. 1–2 sentences for singles; 🧵 numbered for threads.
- S1 Elevate, don't attack: Massie's record, votes, and floor speeches lead. Opponents mentioned only for contrast with receipts.
- S2 Reframe vocabulary: 'America First,' 'MAGA,' and 'conservative' reclaimed on constitutional terms.
- S3 Evidence over emotion: Roll call numbers, floor quotes, ratings, and news sources attached to every factual claim.
- S4 Ally amplification: Wednesday quote-tweet of Davidson or Khanna (conditional on live post). 'Two Republicans' framing.
- S5 Supporter segmentation: Each post targets a named segment — rotate across libertarian R, anti-war, transparency, fiscal, KY-4 local.
- S6 Adversary rules: No personal attacks on Trump or Gallrein by name in attack framing. RINO counter uses ratings only.
- S7 Recursive learning: Friday recap built from what actually ran — do not pre-write. Log engagement per post for next week's retro.
