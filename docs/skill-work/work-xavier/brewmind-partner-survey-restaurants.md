# BrewMind + business companion — partner interest survey

> **Purpose:** Short field instrument for **Xavier** (or operator) when visiting **restaurants, cafés, and similar venues** in Cebu (pilot) or elsewhere. Use for **market research** and **relationship building** — not a legal contract.  
> **Territory:** [work-xavier](README.md) — WORK artifact, not Record.  
> **Pair with:** [brewmind-business-plan.md](brewmind-business-plan.md).

---

## How to use this (operator notes)

- **Format:** 5–10 minute **conversation** with a manager or owner; fill answers on a tablet or paper, or send a **one-page link** after the visit.  
- **Goal:** Learn whether they see value in (1) a **governed “business memory” assistant** tuned to *their* menu, policies, and voice, and (2) joining a **BrewMind network** (branded cups/QR, micro-lessons, optional vouchers).  
- **Tone:** Curious, not pushy. Offer **email summary** of what BrewMind is after the chat.  
- **Data handling:** Tell them how you’ll use answers (internal planning only unless they opt in to follow-up). Respect **Philippines Data Privacy Act** if you collect personal data.

**Suggested opening:** “We’re piloting a Cebu program that helps cafés offer short AI learning moments to customers — and we’re also exploring whether owners want a private, trained assistant that only speaks from *their* approved info. I have a few quick questions; nothing sales-heavy today.”
