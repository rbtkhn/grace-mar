## Onboarding phases (suggested)

### Phase 0 — Align (before printing anything)

- [ ] Operator and Xavier agree: **partner-network pilot** vs **owned shop** (default in plan: **partners first**).
- [ ] Read **bundle map** above; bookmark **public site** for demos on phone.
- [ ] Confirm **no** grace-mar private paths or Abby Record copy in any partner-facing PDF ([LEAKAGE-CHECKLIST.md](LEAKAGE-CHECKLIST.md)).

### Phase 1 — Materials

- [ ] **Pamphlet** (print): follows [brewmind-visual-style.md](brewmind-visual-style.md); one clear CTA (waitlist / QR / email).
- [ ] **QR landing** (minimal): matches brand colors/type; privacy note if you collect email/phone (**Data Privacy Act** — keep statements honest and short).
- [ ] Optional: **Taglish** or Filipino subtitles on pamphlet if primary venues are student-heavy (English still works in many Cebu BPO-adjacent contexts — **test with first 3 owners**).

### Phase 2 — Field (Cebu density pilot)

- [ ] Choose **one cluster** (e.g. IT Park, university strip) — see business plan.
- [ ] Visit **5–8** venues with **pamphlet + five questions** ([brewmind-partner-survey-restaurants.md](brewmind-partner-survey-restaurants.md)); light internal notes only.
- [ ] Track: who allowed follow-up, who asked about **B2B workshops** (feed AI Learning Plan narrative).

### Phase 3 — Learn and iterate

- [ ] Review scan/signup data if QR is live; adjust **incentive** (voucher float) per business plan.
- [ ] Refresh **market research** doc when policy shifts (DepEd AI, NAISR news) — quarterly is enough at seed stage.
- [ ] Optional: pull **1–2 lesson hooks** per week from [work-dev sources](#operator-depth--work-dev-sources-brewmind-bundle) (translate for local audience; keep claims modest).

### Phase 4 — Optional expansion

- [ ] **B2B** half-days (AI Learning Plan) once field proves interest.
- [ ] **Companion-self** upsell for Elite/partners — only with clear **portability** and **human gate** story (see business plan “optional moat”).

## Philippines reminders (one screen)

- **Languages:** Professional AI training online is often **English**; mass social and schools use **Filipino / Taglish** — match the **venue**, not a single rule.
- **Trust:** Many Filipinos first hear “AI” via **viral / deepfake / influencer** news — lead with **human venue, approved info, ethics** (see market research).
- **Policy tailwind:** National AI roadmap + DepEd AI programs = **familiarity**, not automatic demand — you still win on **habit and place**.
