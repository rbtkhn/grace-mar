## What we provide

- **Method:** **Polyphonic cognition protocol** — structured training and cheat sheet for when to query, how to read A/B/C and tensions, relay-only rule. See [cheat-sheet-polyphonic-cognition-protocol.md](../work-politics/cheat-sheet-polyphonic-cognition-protocol.md).
- **Assistant brain (STATE-evolved):** A persistent decision-orientation layer that surfaces **options** through **multiple fixed perspectives** (e.g. legitimacy/orientation, structure/constraints, liability/mechanism), preserves tensions between perspectives, and **never writes to the principal's or institution's record** unless the user explicitly invokes relay ("save," "record," "add to log").
- **Polyphonic cognition:** The product embodies **polyphonic cognition** — multiple perspectives held at once, no single resolution. Fixed slots (A/B/C, optionally D) ensure the same angles are always available; duty of competence ensures the option set includes the full range, including accommodation/reversal options.
- **Optional civ-mem lens:** When the institution has ingested the relevant corpus, one perspective can be the "civ-mem lens" (condition, seam, one subject many tongues, purpose of history) — as one angle among others, not the only one.
- **Deployment:** Secure; on-prem or fed-approved cloud. Inputs stay inside the government boundary; no training on government data without explicit contract and consent.

---

## Why it's unique

- **We never give you one answer:** Typical decision-support tools recommend or converge. This one **only surfaces options and perspectives**; the principal decides. Sovereign remains the user.
- **Tensions preserved; contradiction visible:** Conflicting perspectives (e.g. legitimacy vs structure) are presented and **not resolved**. When two perspectives conflict we label it explicitly ("Tension: A and C conflict here"); the system does not collapse to a single "answer."
- **Sovereign session; explicit relay only:** Your session is sovereign. No silent logging, no training on your data, no retention unless you say "record." Nothing is written to long-term or institutional record unless the user explicitly invokes relay.
- **Fixed perspectives:** Named, stable slots (legitimacy, structure, liability; optional civ-mem lens) so the user always knows what "A/B/C" means. Implementation derived from a proven three-mind (polyphonic cognition) design used in civilizational analysis.
- **Instinct-as-input (optional):** The principal can state their leaning first; the system then shows the full field and where that instinct sits relative to A/B/C — making "testing your judgment" literal.
- **Research alignment:** Aligned with prescriptive AI research on auditing human judgment in high-stakes environments: test judgment, don't replace it (e.g. arXiv 2512.04480).
- **No model branding:** UI and outputs show only "A / B / C" and content — no model names. Focus on structure; reduce vendor lock-in.

## Who it's for

- **Use cases:** Pre-meeting orientation, pre-decision briefs, "what are the options through these lenses?" in the flow of work.
