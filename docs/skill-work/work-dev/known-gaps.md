## Triage rule

Fix provenance before chasing higher-level benchmark or workflow polish. Without provenance, the rest of the territory becomes harder to trust.

**Six-layer priorities (from [positioning-governed-state-os.md](positioning-governed-state-os.md)):** Capability contracts (GAP-008) are most useful at current companion scale. Compute ledger extension (GAP-009) serves operator transparency now and extracts cleanly later. Sandbox adapter (GAP-010) is lowest urgency — park until external sandbox use is imminent.
