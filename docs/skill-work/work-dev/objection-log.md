## Likely early objections

| Objection | What it may mean |
|----------|-------------------|
| **This sounds too abstract** | The offer is too architecture-first and not tied to a concrete pain |
| **We already use vendor X** | The wedge needs to be framed as portability/control, not vendor replacement for its own sake |
| **We just need automation, not governance** | The offer may need to lead with operational friction rather than policy language |
| **We are too early for this** | The buyer may not yet feel enough sprawl or risk to care |
| **This sounds like extra complexity** | The engagement needs to look smaller, clearer, and more bounded |
