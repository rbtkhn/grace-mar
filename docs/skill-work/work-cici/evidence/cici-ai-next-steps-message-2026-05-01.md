## Context

This message explains the community path in four parts: personal OB1 work, Cici as a guide/reference project, evidence-backed learning/building, and member-driven suggestions. It is a framing post, not an intake post.

## Message text

```text
Next Steps — What We’re Building Together

Exciting things ahead. Here is the simple picture of where this community is going.

1. Your Own OB1

OB1 is a personal AI workspace: a repo-shaped assistant you can configure, improve, and use for your own goals.

Each member will work toward their own OB1 setup: your GitHub, your files, your keys, your progress. This is how you learn the real skill, not just watch someone else use AI.

2. Cici — Our Community Guide

Cici is the group’s reference project and learning path. She helps us organize the work, explain steps, draft checklists, and make progress easier to see.

Cici can help us create clear rubrics for learning and contribution, but final decisions stay with people. The goal is fairness, transparency, and visible evidence — not mystery judgment.

3. Learn, Build, Show Proof

Our goal is to prove that a small, focused, AI-assisted community can learn fast and build real things.

We will document progress, share artifacts, help each other through stuck points, and use that evidence to open doors to scholarships, grants, donations, investment, or future work opportunities.

4. Your Voice Matters

Anyone can suggest what we should build, improve, or study next. We will evaluate ideas by usefulness, effort, fairness, and evidence.

This is not just a chat group. It is a learning cohort, a build space, and a case study.

In short:

- Build your own OB1
- Use Cici as the guide and reference path
- Share visible progress
- Help others get unstuck
- Use evidence to earn bigger opportunities

Start thinking about your personal goals. More soon.
```
