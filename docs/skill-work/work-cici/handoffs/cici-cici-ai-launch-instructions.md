# Cici — `cici-ai` Telegram launch tasks (instructions)

**Audience:** Cici (companion instance). **Where this lives:** grace-mar `work-cici` **WORK** — not your sovereign Record; do not treat this file alone as partner-facing product truth.

These two pieces of work matter because the **cici-ai** group still needs a real **front door**: a **pinned** message (rules and norms) plus a **first welcome** that everyone can see, and a **recognizable group avatar** so the room looks intentional and the same mark can follow you to other social profiles without a second branding pass. The operator’s framing for the community push lives in [cici-community-mission-operator.md](../cici-community-mission-operator.md#near-term-actions-cici).

The group is **cici-ai** on Telegram, which you created. If you re-share a join link, use only the **t.me** URL recorded in the evidence note [cici-telegram-cici-ai-group-operator-2026-04-25.md](../evidence/cici-telegram-cici-ai-group-operator-2026-04-25.md); do not invent a new link.

**Before you start**, confirm you are **owner or admin** of **cici-ai** so you can **pin** messages and **change the group photo**. Open [telegram-cici-ai-pinned-and-welcome-copy.md](../telegram-cici-ai-pinned-and-welcome-copy.md) and read **§0** once, so the words **Open Brain (OB1)**, the **purpose of the group**, and the **golden-path repo** are clear in your head before you paste. Your default text is the **§1** and **§2** blocks in that file. If a client forces you to shorten, you still must keep the **name Open Brain (OB1)**, the **purpose of the group**, and the URL **https://github.com/Xavier-x01/Cici**. If you want to change meaning or tone beyond a light trim, check with the operator first.

**Task 1 — pin and first welcome, in order.** The point is that every member, now and later, can open the chat and see **what the group is for** and **how to behave** in a pinned line, and that early members are not met with silence. In **cici-ai**, take the text from **§1 — Pinned message** in the paste pack (the block inside the ` ```text ` fence), send it as a normal message, then use the message menu to **pin** it to the top and confirm the pin is visible. Next, send the **§2 — Introductory message** from the same file, with the **@all** (or your client’s equivalent) line first if it helps visibility, and use whatever “notify all members” action your app gives admins. If you cannot trigger a global notify, still send the message and let the operator know so they can **@** the room or make a one-time announcement. Optionally, set the **group description** (About) to a one-line summary that matches the pin: OB1, hobbyist mutual help, and the Cici repo as the golden path. You are finished with this task when a **pinned** post with the §1 content sits at the top, the **first welcome** (§2) appears in the history with everyone notified if your permissions allow it, and either you or the operator has dropped **one line** of proof for the advisor trail— for example in grace-mar [cici-progress-log.md](../cici-progress-log.md) or in your own `docs/operator-daily-log.md` if you use that. **Leakage:** never paste API keys, tokens, or raw `.env` into Telegram or public GitHub; if you use a screenshot, redact secrets.

**Task 2 — logo and group avatar** can start as soon as Task 1 is under way, not only after it closes. The goal is a **group photo** that still reads in Telegram’s **small circular** crop, and a **master file** you can reuse elsewhere without redesigning. Pick a **concept** with a short, memorable link to **cici-ai** and to **Open Brain / building**; favor clarity at the size of a **chat list icon** (on the order of 48px), not a poster. Build a **square** master (for example 1024×1024 or 512×512 pixels) and keep the important mark in the **center ~70%** of the frame, because Telegram will crop a **circle**. Export a **PNG** for the group picture; keep the **editable** source (Figma, SVG, layered file) in **your** repo or private storage unless the operator asks to mirror something in grace-mar. In Telegram, open **cici-ai** → group settings → **Edit** → set the **Group photo** from that PNG, then check on a **phone** how it looks in the chat list, where the avatar is very small. If you can, also save a **wider** or **wordmark** layout for a future “X / YouTube / Locals” header using the same colors and mark, so you are extending one identity instead of starting a new one. This task is **done** when the group shows the **new photo** in search and the chat list, you can still **reproduce** the asset from a saved project for other platforms, and (optionally) you have one line in your daily log or a short note to the operator that the **group avatar** went live on a given date. If design time is tight, a **plain interim** (for example initials on one color) is acceptable until a final mark ships: almost any intentional icon is better than the **default empty** bubble for trust.

**If something goes wrong:** without pin rights, ask the **owner** for **admin** or have them **pin** for you. If **@all** is unavailable, post §2 anyway and ask the operator to announce. If a block is too long for one Telegram message, split **§2** into two parts on the same day and keep **§1** as a single pin if you can. This note is the procedure; the paste pack is the **exact words** unless the operator approves a change. Escalate to the operator or the usual advisor channel when blockers are not on this list.
