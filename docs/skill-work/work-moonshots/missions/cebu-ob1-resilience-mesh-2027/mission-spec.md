## Mission name

Cebu **OB1 resilience mesh** — coordinated **forked** instances (Cici-style git-first governed layers) to improve MSME economic throughput under **grid uncertainty** and **cost-of-living pressure**, without claiming to operate or fix the public grid.

## Mission statement

Cebu’s economy faces **volatile electricity costs and reliability narratives** (advocacy warnings vs utility assurances) and **persistent poverty pressure** despite **improved headline PSA poverty incidence** in 2023 vs 2021. This mission drafts a **network protocol**: many **owner-governed** OB1 forks in Cebu publish **Tier A** auditable playbooks and **Tier B** prepared market context so small firms reduce downtime and procurement friction. **Non-linear leverage** (if validated) comes from **template reuse + verified supplier edges + placement pathways**—not from a merged “hive mind” or unverified social rumor.

## Success condition

- **Mesh:** ≥ 25 distinct Cebu-area OB1 owners with public alignment to a shared **taxonomy** (incident classes, vendor categories, skills tags) within 24–36 months (target; revise when baselined).
- **MSME outcomes:** ≥ 200 MSMEs complete a structured **resilience kit** with before/after **documented** operating hours or revenue proxy (self-reported but schema-bound).
- **Quality bar:** zero tolerance for **Tier C recall** presented as grid or safety truth; any safety-critical claim carries **official source** tier or is excluded.

## Failure condition

- **Stop** if unverified outage / emergency claims spread from mesh artifacts (reputational or safety tail).
- **Pivot** if **no** credible path to LGU / utility / DOE **information** engagement after two structured cycles—narrow to pure business continuity (no “grid truth” posture).
- **Stop** pending counsel if coordination drifts toward **price coordination** that could resemble bid-rigging or cartel behavior.
- **Park** if participant time-on-tool exceeds agreed caps with no economic outcome for two consecutive review quarters.

## Time horizon

24–36 months to first **reviewed** network effects; 90-day **Phase 1** vertical wedge optional (see phases below).

## Constraints

- **Governance:** each instance remains **owner-approved** for governed changes; no cross-write to others’ repos.
- **Privacy / law:** no pooled PII; group-buy or shared procurement only after legal review.
- **Epistemic:** competing public narratives on Visayas supply (advocacy vs distributors vs DOE statements) must be **labeled**, not flattened into a single “truth.”

## Forbidden shortcuts

- Scraping or centralizing personal data without consent.
- Automated posting of incidents or outages.
- Presenting assistant retrieval as **approval** for promotions or safety decisions.
- Claiming “exponential GDP impact” without **defined** metrics and receipts.
