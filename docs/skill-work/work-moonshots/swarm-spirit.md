# Swarm spirit — PMOS (WORK ritual, not automation)

**WORK only.** This document names a **human-operated** pattern: many **modes of attention**, one **reviewable** bundle before anything durable. It does **not** describe daemons, bot commands, or scripts that write to [`recursion-gate.md`](../../../users/grace-mar/recursion-gate.md) or the Record.

**One-line distillation:** *Many voices in the workroom, one envelope at the door.*

---

## What this is for

Splitting judgment so one pass does not have to hold research, imagination, execution, and skepticism at once—then **converging** into something small enough to **review once** at the gate.

Differentiation → integration: separate **modes**, run each in a **bounded** way, then **weave** into a **single** proposal when you choose to promote something.

---

## Grace-Mar-native mapping

| Informal role (spirit) | Native expression |
|------------------------|-------------------|
| “Sub-agents” | **Session roles** or **lenses**—labels for how you draft or read in one sitting (or a few). Not autonomous writers. |
| “Coordinator” | **You**, or a **checklist** in this lane / a [mission](missions/README.md) folder—not a background process. |
| “Parallel work” | **Parallel notes** (headings, scratch under [tacit-capture](../../tacit-capture/README.md) runtime, mission `intake/`), merged by hand. |
| “Synthesizer” | **Integration pass**: one pass that turns fragments into **one** gate-ready block—still **human-triggered**. |
| “Experts” | **Borrow a lens**: strategy [experts](../work-strategy/strategy-notebook/experts/) threads, tri-frame when explicitly invoked—reading and citation, not spawned processes under a fake `experts/<id>/` tree. |

**Membrane:** WORK and runtime hold drafts and weather; **SELF / EVIDENCE / SKILLS / prompt** change only through the **existing** gate + merge path ([AGENTS.md](../../../AGENTS.md)).

---

## A round (sequential, human-sized)

Use as a **menu**, not a mandatory pipeline—skip or repeat steps as needed.

1. **Gather** — What is known vs claimed? (Links, quotes, tacit captures.)
2. **Stress** — What breaks this if the world is worse than hoped? What would falsify it?
3. **Commit** — What is the **next** n=1 experiment or artifact?
4. **Audit** — Who is harmed if this is wrong? What does the Record **not** yet support?
5. **Weave** — One short page: claim, limits, next step, and—if applicable—**shape** for a `CANDIDATE-*` (you still paste or stage by hand).

**Scope rule:** give each step a **stop rule** (e.g. one screen, three bullets, one risk paragraph).

---

## What this rejects

- **Appending** to `recursion-gate.md` from scripts or “swarm runners.”
- **Implying** EVIDENCE or SELF updates without companion-approved merge.
- **Pretending** bot or CLI commands exist before they are explicitly built and governed.
