## Formal job description

### Title

Social Media Manager — Shadow Campaign (X / Twitter)

### Summary

Operate the X (Twitter) account **@usa_first_ky** as **unofficial independent analysis & message support** for Rep. Thomas Massie (R-KY-4). The account provides analysis, context, and message support — not as the official campaign, and not as an office. The role implements a defined X strategy through the KY-4 primary (May 19, 2026), with content drafted by an AI agent and reviewed and posted by the SMM.

### Responsibilities

- **Execute X strategy:** Respond to Massie's posts; quote and amplify his content; increase engagement and follower count for the principal; sway public opinion in a favorable direction; demonstrate principal's strengths (positions, record, message discipline).
- **Content operations:** Review drafts provided by the agent; edit for voice and authenticity; post to @usa_first_ky. Ensure tone is professional, evidence-based, and pro-principal without partisan slash-and-burn.
- **Voice calibration:** Maintain consistency with the principal's verified X voice (direct, declarative, principle-first). Use provided training materials for authenticity checks.
- **Recursive learning:** Observe what works; feed back into the loop to improve future drafts and engagement.
- **Compliance:** Post only reviewed content; no autonomous posting. Do not make promises or commitments on behalf of the principal or campaign. Cite sources; correct the record when misattributed.

### Qualifications

- Experience with X (Twitter) content strategy and community management.
- Ability to match voice and tone to a documented persona.
- Comfort with AI-drafted content and iterative editing.
- Familiarity with U.S. political context (Kentucky 4th District, congressional races) helpful but not required.

### Compensation

- **Base:** 0.1 BTC for implementing the X plan through the election (KY-4 primary May 19, 2026).
- **Bonus:** 0.1 BTC if the principal wins the primary.
- **Total potential:** 0.2 BTC.

### Duration

Through KY-4 primary, May 19, 2026. Scope may extend or pivot based on outcome.

### Reporting

Reports to the companion (operator). Receives drafts from the agent; coordinates requests and feedback through the companion or directly as agreed.
