# WAP compliance checklist (pre-engagement)

**Not legal advice.** Operator + qualified counsel answer these before paid work or public attribution. Repo tracks **gates only**; answers live outside git if sensitive.

---

## US — federal

| # | Question | Clear before |
|---|----------|--------------|
| 1 | Is the engagement **FEC-regulated** (federal candidate, PAC, party)? | Federal deliverables |
| 2 | Who is the **official client of record** for disclosure (campaign, committee, vendor)? | Contract |
| 3 | Any **coordination** rules implicated (IE vs coordinated)? | Paid media / message |

Pointers: FEC.gov; campaign counsel.

---

## US — state / local

| # | Question | Clear before |
|---|----------|--------------|
| 4 | **State registration** required for paid political consulting / lobbying? | State deliverables |
| 5 | **Local** disclosure (city/county ethics, lobbyist)? | Local deliverables |
| 6 | Gift / revolving-door / contractor rules for this jurisdiction? | If interacting with officeholders |

Pointers: state ethics commission; municipal clerk.

---

## US — foreign principal (FARA angle)

| # | Question | Clear before |
|---|----------|--------------|
| 7 | Is the principal a **foreign government, party, or person** directing US political messaging? | Any US-facing work for foreign client |
| 8 | Does activity fall under **FARA** or DOJ registration? | Before taking foreign-directed US work |

Pointers: Justice.gov FARA; counsel.

---

## International (non-US principal or non-US election)

| # | Question | Clear before |
|---|----------|--------------|
| 9 | **Country** + **election or issue** — local law on **paid advocacy**, foreign consultants, data, ads? | Before scoping call billable |
| 10 | US person **export / sanctions** touch any party or payment rail? | Before contract |
| 11 | Language / jurisdiction of **contract and dispute**? | Before signature |

**Gate:** Do not list “international” on [clients/_template.md](clients/_template.md) as active until rows 9–11 have operator sign-off + date.
