## Weekly brief template (demo)

1. **Headline reality** — One paragraph: race position (primary clock, money, noise in CA-17).
2. **Votes / record** — 2–3 bullets + one local explanation line each (e.g. how Iran vote lands for CA commuters or tech workers).
3. **Earned + social** — What broke in Bay Area / national media; repeat or drop.
4. **Opposition** — Agarwal + tech donor moves; **pre-buttal** (short, factual).
5. **Fundraising** — Week theme + one “why give now” that isn’t just “protect the incumbent,” but ties to worker/anti-capture framing.
6. **Field / local** — Union, community, campus, and tech-worker notes; one ask per segment if useful.
7. **Risks** — Missteps; compliance or ethical flags (e.g. trading optics).
8. **Next week** — Three numbered human-owned priorities.

Align loosely with Massie’s [generate_wap_weekly_brief.py](../../../../scripts/generate_wap_weekly_brief.py) rhythm; this is a demo lane, not a production brief unless the companion chooses to use it.
