# SMM training curriculum — @usa_first_ky

**Audience:** New social media manager (Xavier) and anyone shadowing X ops.  
**Companion use:** Assign modules by week; use “show you know” checks before expanding posting authority.

---

## Learning outcomes (end of onboarding)

By the end of **Week 2**, the SMM can:

1. **Explain** who **Thomas Massie** and **Ro Khanna** are in plain terms, and how **US House** elections and **primary vs general** work — **without assuming prior American civics knowledge** (see [smm-training.md](smm-training.md) § US politics + Who is Massie/Khanna).
2. **State** the account’s legal/ethical positioning (unofficial analysis; not the principal, not the campaign/office) and apply it to every post.
3. **Run** the draft review loop: agent or advisor produces copy → SMM checks voice + sourcing + doctrine → SMM publishes (no auto-post).
4. **Use** Massie’s documented record and voice patterns (principal-profile, smm-training) without inventing positions.
5. **Route** high-stakes topics (war powers, Epstein/DOJ, hot Trump window) through the stress-test path when the companion requires it.
6. **Read** the work-politics client doctrine (KY-4 neighbor sentence; engineer/farmer; anti-waste / anti-surveillance / anti-stupid-war) and reject drafts that violate it.

## Order of operations (recommended)

1. Complete **M0** same day as access handoff.  
2. **M1–M2** before first unsupervised post.  
3. **M3–M4** before first reply to a high-attention account or first thread on war/Epstein.  
4. **M5** by end of Week 1.  
5. Revisit **M2** monthly or after any voice miss (bad RT ratio, correction needed, companion flag).
