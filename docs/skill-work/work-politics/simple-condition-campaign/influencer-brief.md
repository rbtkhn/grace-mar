# Influencer Brief — Simple Condition Iran Campaign

**1–2 pager:** thesis summary, campaign ask, what to post. Human approval required before distribution.

---

## What This Is

A viral campaign to end the Iran war by (1) individuating Iranians ("see the face"), (2) framing Congress vote as the coordination focal point, and (3) speaking to secular and religious audiences with the same core message. The narrative backbone is the **Simple Condition** — peace as condition, not consequence; the blade vs. beauty; coordination needs a focal point.

---

## Thesis Summary (30 seconds)

**The Simple Condition** argues:

1. **Peace is the condition** — not the reward. The "door is open"; we're the ones holding it shut.
2. **See the face** — killing requires dehumanisation. Individuation (names, faces, stories) reverses it.
3. **Coordination needs a focal point** — the Congress vote on war powers is that focal moment. "Demand the vote."
4. **Every tradition carries beauty and a blade** — the seam is visible. Choose the beauty; put down the blade.

**For Iran:** We're not pro-regime. We're anti-killing. Iranian people have names and faces. Congress must decide whether we go to war — your representative needs to be on record. Demand the vote.

---

## Campaign Ask

**What we're asking you to do:**

1. **Post** — Share one or more of: a one-liner from the library, a "see the face" asset (Iranian story/photo), or a "demand the vote" CTA.
2. **Timing** — Align with the focal event (next Congress vote or anniversary — see calendar).
3. **Tone** — In-character. No need to endorse theology; secular framing works ("coordination needs a focal point," "demand the vote").
4. **CTA** — Direct people to contact their rep, sign the clergy letter (if faith leader), or share the content.

**What we're NOT asking:** Endorsement of any government. Partisan branding. Anything that could read "pro-Iran regime."
