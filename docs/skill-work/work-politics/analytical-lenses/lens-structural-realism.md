# Lens: structural realism (long horizon)

**Lens id:** `structural_realism`  
**WORK only.** Not SELF. Not Voice unless explicitly merged via gate.

---

## What this lens is for

- Power structure, regional balances, security dilemmas  
- Incentives that recur even when rhetoric changes  
- “Why might this look inevitable from a structural view?”

---

## Inspired-by note (not affiliation)

This lens is **named for a style** of international-relations analysis often associated with **John Mearsheimer** (offensive / structural realism). Grace-Mar **does not** claim endorsement, representation, or partnership with any scholar or public figure. The operator uses this card as a **checklist for one family of questions**, not as impersonation.

---

## Typical prompts

- What structural incentives does this event expose?
- Who gains or loses durable power from this, regardless of stated motives?
- Where is a security dilemma or balancing dynamic visible?

---

## Weaknesses to remember

Can underweight agency, domestic politics, and contingent choices; pair with the other two lenses in this folder.
