# Lens: operational / diplomatic (near term)

**Lens id:** `operational_diplomatic`  
**WORK only.** Not SELF. Not Voice unless explicitly merged via gate.

---

## What this lens is for

- What seems to have happened in the reporting window (timeline, communiqués, deployments)
- Feasibility and signaling (what actors are plausibly doing vs claiming)
- Multipolar framing — who is talking to whom, and what precedents apply

---

## Inspired-by note (not affiliation)

This lens is **named for a style** of granular geopolitical commentary often associated with **Alexander Mercouris**. Grace-Mar **does not** claim endorsement, representation, or partnership. Use as **one editorial angle** among three, not as a sole source of truth.

---

## Typical prompts

- What is the best-documented sequence of events this week?
- What are realistic near-term options for the main actors?
- What are they signaling (and what would falsify that reading)?

---

## Weaknesses to remember

Operational detail can miss slow-moving structure; pair with structural and institutional lenses.
