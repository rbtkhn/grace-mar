## Blog: When Your AI Companion Ghosts You (Nov 5, 2025)

**Author:** Zoë Covington-Towner, Director of Growth  
**URL:** https://www.uare.ai/blog/when-your-ai-companion-ghosts-you-and-why-thats-a-problem

**Excerpts:**
> We're starting to build real relationships with AI, but what happens when the one that understands you suddenly disappears?
>
> … You've spent months with your AI assistant. It knows your quirks, remembers your stories, gets your humor. You've built something that feels... real. Then boom. "We're deprecating this model. Meet your new assistant!" … Your data might transfer. But the personality? The vibe? The way it understood you? Often gone.
>
> **Why This Matters:** Legacy is at stake. If your AI holds your life story, what happens when the model dies? … Your "digital you" gets scrambled. Even if your data survives, how it's used can change completely.
>
> **What We're Doing About It at Uare.ai:** You own your data. Not us. Not the model. You. … We're model-agnostic. One LLM won't rule forever, so we build for migration and evolution. We design for decades, not months. Your AI twin should outlive the hype cycle.
>
> As the AI companion wave grows, companies need to answer: Are you building relationships that last, or just features that get deprecated? At Uare.ai, we're betting on the relationship.

## Blog: Your AI Should Sound Like You, Not a Robot (Dec 19, 2025)

**Author:** Serge-Eric Tremblay, Head of Product  
**URL:** https://www.uare.ai/blog/your-ai-should-sound-like-you-not-a-robot

**Excerpts:**
> Most AI today is good at sounding human-ish—friendly, polished, a little bit bland. … But that's not the same as sounding like you. … Uare.ai is taking a different path: instead of just humanizing AI, we're building Individual AIs that are rooted in your lived experience, so the content doesn't just sound human, it sounds like your voice.
>
> "Humanized" AI without identity is still generic. It may be nicer to read, but it doesn't carry your history, your inside jokes, or the way you soften hard feedback with one specific phrase you always use.
>
> At the core of Uare.ai is the Human Life Model—a framework for building an Individual AI from your stories, memories, preferences, and patterns of thought, so it can think and respond the way you do. … Instead of training on the entire internet, your Individual AI is built only on your data: The way you encourage, push, reassure, or challenge; your recorded stories and reflections; your emails, talks, and drafts. On top of that, Uare.ai's Neural Voice technology can generate speech modeled on your tone, cadence, and expression.
>
> [We] pair emotional realism with strong boundaries: your data is containerized, never trained into public models, and your Individual AI is clearly presented as an AI—powered by you, not pretending to be you.
>
> The goal is personal presence: an AI that carries your voice into more rooms than you can physically be in, without losing the nuance that makes you, you. … Not just giving it a warmer tone, but giving it a soulful outline of the person it represents, that is anchored in your values, your memories, and yes, your unmistakable voice.

## Press release: Human Life Model (HLM) and positioning (BusinessWire, Nov 11, 2025)

**Excerpts:**
> Uare.ai … announced it has raised $10.3 million in seed funding to accelerate the development of Individual AIs - digital counterparts that harness a person's memories, stories, expertise, and voice that mirror how you think, speak, and make decisions.
>
> Unlike much of the AI industry, which is chasing AGI — one model to serve everyone- Uare.ai is pioneering Individual AI. Each model is private, containerized, and self-owned. It evolves alongside you, supports creativity and mentorship, and provides context-aware decision-making, all while keeping your memories, business plans, and personal data secure.
>
> The company's Human Life Model (HLM) is its key strategic differentiator. This proprietary framework encodes a person's stories, values, voice, and decision-making patterns, so their Individual AI doesn't just repeat what they've said; it reasons and responds as they would. Unlike wrappers on large language models, Uare.ai creates high-fidelity replicas that admit when they don't know an answer instead of guessing.

## Use for grace-mar positioning

- **Shared ground:** User-owned, evidence-from-you-only, anti-hallucination ("I don't know"), identity that outlives model churn, values and voice.
- **Differentiation:** Open repo, portable PRP/.grace, explicit human gate (RECURSION-GATE), no vendor lock-in, optional local/offline, developer- and research-friendly; Uare is proprietary SaaS, no public export format, no visible gated-merge workflow.
- **Pitch angle:** "The market is validating individual AI (Uare $10M+, Mayfield/Boldstart). We're the open, gated, portable version—your Record lives in your repo, you approve every change, and you can export it anywhere."
