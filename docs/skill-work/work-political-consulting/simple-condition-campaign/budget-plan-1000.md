# Simple Condition Public Awareness — $1,000 Budget Plan

**Scope:** Allocate a $1,000 budget across the [public awareness campaign](public-awareness-campaign-design.md) to maximize reach and recognition of the Simple Condition. Assumes operator/volunteer time for copy, outreach, and organic posting; money buys assets, distribution, and limited paid amplification.

**Governance:** Human approval required for spend. Per [civ-mem-draft-protocol.md](../civ-mem-draft-protocol.md).

---

## Budget summary

| Category | Allocation | Purpose |
|----------|------------|---------|
| **Assets** | $400 | One-pager + letter summary design; 3–5 quote graphics; optional simple video |
| **Distribution** | $350 | Letter mail-out to religious leaders; or email tool + printing for in-person handouts |
| **Paid amplification** | $150 | Small paid social boost (Meta, X, or both) to test what resonates |
| **Reserve / contingency** | $100 | Unplanned (e.g. translation of one language, extra print run, small fee for teacher one-pager design) |

**Total:** $1,000

---

## 1. Assets ($400)

| Line item | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| **One-pager + letter summary (2–3 pp) design** | $150–200 | Freelancer (Fiverr, Upwork, or design-for-good) to turn copy into a clean, printable PDF and a 2–3 page letter summary. Operator supplies text from existing essays. |
| **Quote graphics (3–5)** | $100–150 | Same freelancer or batch: 3–5 shareable quote cards (e.g. "The door is open. We're the ones holding it shut."; "Every tradition carries beauty and a blade."; "One subject, many tongues."). Consistent type and layout for social and print. |
| **Simple video (90 sec or less)** | $50–100 | If budget allows: stock footage + text overlay + voiceover (operator or volunteer), or a single static graphic with captions edited in CapCut/Canva. No full production. Otherwise $0 and do text-only or slideshow in-house. |

**Free / in-house:** One-line library (adapt from existing); pulpit reading and faith-communities kit (done); teacher one-pager *copy* (operator); op-ed drafts (operator). No spend on these.

---

## 2. Distribution ($350)

| Line item | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Letter mail-out** | $200–250 | Print 2–3 page letter summary + one-pager (100–150 sets). Mail to 50–75 religious leaders or institutions (US or target region). Envelope + postage ~$1.50–2.50 per piece. Prioritize: denominational offices, seminary deans, interfaith councils, 1–2 key clergy per tradition. |
| **Print for in-person** | $50–75 | Extra one-pagers for conferences, church handouts, campus tables. 100–200 copies at print shop or online (e.g. VistaPrint, local). |
| **Email / tooling** | $0–50 | If mail is skipped: Mailchimp or similar free tier to send letter summary + link to full letter to a curated list. Or $0 and use existing email. |

**Alternative:** If operator prefers digital-only for leaders: spend the $250 on a larger print run for in-person distribution (500+ one-pagers) and use email for the letter. Reallocate from "Letter mail-out" to "Print for in-person" and "Paid amplification."

---

## 3. Paid amplification ($150)

| Line item | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Meta (Facebook/Instagram) boost** | $75–100 | Boost 1–2 posts: quote graphic or short video + link to letter/summary. Target: interest in religion, peace, interfaith, philosophy. $5–10/day for 7–10 days or one 1-week boost. Goal: test resonance; capture emails or follows if CTA included. |
| **X (Twitter) promote** | $50–75 | Promote one tweet with key quote + link. Short run (2–3 days). Or skip and put full $150 into Meta for one stronger test. |

**Goal:** Learn which message (door is open, beauty and blade, one subject many tongues) or format (quote vs video) gets engagement. Not scale — signal.

---

## 4. Reserve ($100)

- **Translation:** Pay a qualified translator for one language (e.g. Spanish or Arabic) for the one-pager or letter summary — ~$50–80 for 2–3 pages.
- **Extra design:** One more asset (e.g. teacher one-pager layout, second set of graphics).
- **Unexpected print or postage:** Larger list, reprint.
- **Small fee:** E.g. submission fee for a conference, or small donation to an interfaith org in exchange for sharing the letter with their list (only if aligned and approved).

---

## 5. What stays free (no budget)

- All copy and strategy (operator / volunteer).
- Organic social posting (operator / volunteers).
- Pitching op-eds and essays (no pay-for-play; pitch on merit).
- Clergy and educator outreach (email, DMs, existing relationships).
- Use of existing campaign docs: pulpit reading, faith-communities kit, one-line library (awareness version).
- Hosting: letter and one-pager on existing site or GitHub Pages; no new domain or hosting cost in this plan.

---

## 6. Phasing the spend

| Phase | When | Spend |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Foundation** | Weeks 1–2 | Assets: design one-pager + letter summary + quote graphics ($250–350). Reserve video or do in-house. |
| **Seed** | Weeks 3–5 | Distribution: print + mail or print + email ($250–350). Post graphics organically. |
| **Amplify** | Weeks 5–8 | Paid boost ($150). Use best-performing asset. Reserve for translation or extra print if results justify. |
| **Reserve** | As needed | Translation, extra design, or contingency. |

---

## 7. Success with $1K

- **Realistic:** 50–75 physical letter/summary mailings; 100–200 one-pagers in circulation; 3–5 professional quote graphics and one strong one-pager; one small paid test; optional one-language translation.
- **Stretch:** If design is done in-house and mail is replaced by email, reallocate to more paid ($300–400) or to translation of 2 languages.
- **Metric:** Track opens/shares (email), engagement on boosted post, and any clergy or educator replies. Quality of response matters more than raw reach at this budget.

---

*Human approval required before committing spend. Adjust allocations to match operator priorities (e.g. more mail, less paid; or translation first, mail later).*
