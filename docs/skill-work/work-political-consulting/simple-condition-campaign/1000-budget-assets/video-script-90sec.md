# Video Script — 90-Second Simple Condition Awareness

**Purpose:** Short video for organic and paid social. One idea: peace as condition, the seam, or one subject many tongues. No policy or conflict-specific ask. CTA: read the letter.

**Length:** 60–90 seconds. Can be cut to 30 or 45 sec by using only the first block.

**Production note:** Option A — Single quote graphic with captions + voiceover + optional royalty-free music (Canva/CapCut). Option B — Stock footage (e.g. diverse faces, hands, light) + text overlay + voiceover. No full production; keep cost under $50–100 if outsourcing.

---

## Script (voiceover or on-screen captions)

**Block 1 (0:00–0:25)**  
"What if peace isn't the reward we get after something happens — but the condition we have to meet first? A letter to the world's religious leaders asks exactly that. It's called the Simple Condition."

**Block 2 (0:25–0:50)**  
"Every tradition carries beauty — the prayers, the compassion, the vision of a world made whole. And every tradition carries a blade — teachings that have been used to justify violence. The letter doesn't ask anyone to abandon their faith. It asks them to make the seam visible. To see where the beauty ends and the blade begins. So that people can choose."

**Block 3 (0:50–1:15)**  
"We don't need one creed or one language to coordinate. We need one subject — one thing we all point to in our own words. Say it in Hebrew. Say it in Arabic. Say it in the language your people pray in. The subject is the same. When we all say it, we can finally hear each other."

**Block 4 (1:15–1:30)**  
"Read the full letter. Share it with your community. One subject. Many tongues. The door is open."

[CTA on screen: "Read the letter" + URL]

---

## Production checklist

- [ ] Voiceover: operator or volunteer; clear, calm, invitational tone.
- [ ] Music (optional): royalty-free; no lyrics; low volume under voice.
- [ ] Text: key phrases as captions for accessibility and mute viewing.
- [ ] End card: logo or project name + "Read the letter" + URL.
- [ ] Export: 1080×1920 (vertical) for Stories/Reels, and/or 1080×1920 or 16:9 for feed. Under 90 seconds for most platforms.

## Approval

Principal approves final script and final video before any public post.
