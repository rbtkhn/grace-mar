# Designer Brief — Simple Condition Public Awareness Assets

**Project:** Create print and social assets for a public awareness campaign about "The Simple Condition" — a letter to the world's religious leaders about peace as condition, the seam (beauty and blade), and one subject many tongues. Audience: religious and secular; invitational tone; no partisan or sectarian messaging.

**Budget (for freelancer):** $250–350 total (one-pager + letter summary design: $150–200; quote graphics: $100–150, or combined).

---

## Deliverables

1. **One-pager PDF** — Single page (8.5×11 or A4), printable. Content: see attached `one-pager-copy.md`. Clean, readable typography; suitable for handouts and web.
2. **Letter summary PDF** — 2–3 pages (8.5×11 or A4), printable. Content: see attached `letter-summary-copy.md`. Same visual system as one-pager; dignified, readable.
3. **Quote graphics (3–5)** — Shareable image files for social and print. Use the exact lines below. Consistent style across all cards (font, layout, palette). Export as PNG or JPG; for social prefer 1080×1080 (square) and/or 1200×628 (landscape). Print-ready version optional (high-res).

---

## Quote graphics — exact lines

Use these verbatim (one line per graphic, or combine 2 short lines if layout works):

1. **"The door is open. We're the ones holding it shut."**
2. **"Every tradition carries beauty and a blade in the same hand."**
3. **"One subject, many tongues."**
4. **"Does anyone have to die for this to be true? When the answer is no, that's the beauty. Keep it."**
5. **"Peace isn't the reward. Peace is the condition."**

(If only 3 graphics: use #1, #2, #3.)

---

## Style and tone

- **Visual:** Clean, professional, readable. Not gloomy or aggressive. Suitable for religious and secular audiences.
- **Type:** Clear hierarchy; body text readable at print size. No decorative or sectarian imagery.
- **Color:** Neutral or warm; avoid partisan or national associations. Simple palette (e.g. black/dark gray text on light background, or one accent color).
- **Tone:** Invitational, respectful. The campaign is "we are making this available" — not "you have failed."
