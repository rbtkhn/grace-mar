# One-Line Library — Simple Condition Iran Campaign

Shareable lines derived from the Simple Condition thesis. Use for memes, graphics, social posts, and A/B testing. Source: [THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION.md](../../../civilization-memory/essays/THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION.md), [THE-COORDINATION-HYPOTHESIS.md](../../../civilization-memory/essays/THE-COORDINATION-HYPOTHESIS.md).

---

## Peace as condition

1. The door is open. We're the ones holding it shut.
2. Peace isn't the reward. Peace is the condition.
3. What if God doesn't arrive and then establish peace — but returns when we stop killing?
4. Stop killing, and see what happens.
5. The price is not sacrifice. The price is stopping.
6. Every day we keep killing is another day we keep the door closed.
7. The door has been open this entire time.

---

## See the face

8. See the face again.
9. Every life has a name. Every death has a face.
10. They are not targets. They are people.
11. The blade cannot survive contact with a specific human face.
12. Before you call them enemies, see their children. See their parents. See their faces.
13. Dehumanisation is the price of killing. Individuation reverses it.
14. One name. One face. One story. That's how the blade dies.

---

## Coordination / focal point

15. Demand the vote.
16. Congress must decide war.
17. Coordination needs a focal point. The vote is ours.
18. One moment. One decision. One vote. That's how we coordinate.
19. If American lives are to be risked, the people's representatives must decide.
20. The Constitution requires a vote. Your representative needs to be on record.

---

## Blade vs beauty (religious / seam)

21. Your faith says every life is sacred. This war says some lives are targets.
22. Choose the seam. Beauty or blade — you don't have to abandon your faith to put down the weapon.
23. Every tradition carries beauty and a blade in the same hand. Make the seam visible.
24. Does anyone have to die for this to be true? When the answer is no, that's the beauty. Keep it.
25. The sacred and the sword — your tradition has both. Choose.

---

## Waiting / remembrance

26. Every soul who has died is waiting.
27. The dead cannot meet the condition. We can.
28. Every victim of every war you blessed — they're waiting for the living to stop.
29. Their memory is being used to fuel the next round of killing. Don't let the dead be weaponised.
30. We keep killing in their name. They never asked for that.

---

## Bipartisan / exhausted

31. The door is open. We keep closing it.
32. This isn't left or right. It's human or not.
33. Tired of war? The door is open. We're the ones holding it shut.
34. Peace as condition — not naivety. Not weakness. The door is open.

## Usage notes

- **A/B test:** Track which lines drive the most shares, comments, and link-clicks.
- **Format variations:** Try with/without attribution; with/without "Demand the vote" CTA; with/without Iranian face/name.
- **Lexile / accessibility:** Most lines are short and direct; avoid jargon for maximum shareability.
- **Localisation:** Translate top performers into Arabic, Farsi, Hebrew, Spanish for multilingual distribution.
