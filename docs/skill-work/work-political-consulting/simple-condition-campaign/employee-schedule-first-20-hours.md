# Employee Schedule — First 20 Hours

**Purpose:** Hour-by-hour schedule for a human employee executing the Simple Condition public awareness campaign ($1K budget). Tied to [employee-instructions-1000-budget.md](employee-instructions-1000-budget.md) and [budget-plan-1000.md](budget-plan-1000.md). If copy and one-line library already exist in repo, "draft" may mean "review and lightly edit"; if not, draft from source.

**Governance:** Stop at each approval gate and wait for principal sign-off before continuing. If approval is delayed, use the "If waiting" tasks in that block.

---

## Hour 1 — Onboarding and setup

| Task | Action |
|------|--------|
| Read | [budget-plan-1000.md](budget-plan-1000.md), [public-awareness-campaign-design.md](public-awareness-campaign-design.md). Skim [employee-instructions-1000-budget.md](employee-instructions-1000-budget.md) full flow. |
| Confirm | Email or message principal: (1) $1,000 and category allocations approved? (2) Who approves (name) and how (email/Slack/doc)? (3) Where to store deliverables (path or drive)? |
| Locate | Open THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION.md, one-line-library.md, and folder `simple-condition-campaign/` and `1000-budget-assets/`. Note if one-pager-copy, letter-summary-copy, one-line-library-awareness, and designer-brief already exist. |
| Log | Open `1000-budget-assets/spend-log.csv` and `results-log.md`; confirm you can edit. |

**Deliverable:** One-sentence note to principal: "Started; need confirmation on approver and deliverable location."

---

## Hours 2–4 — Copy (Step 0.1)

| Hours | Task | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 2–3 | One-pager copy | If `1000-budget-assets/one-pager-copy.md` exists: read, edit for clarity and length (~300–400 words). If not: draft from THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION and campaign design §3. Sections: What is the Condition; door is open; beauty and blade / seam; one subject many tongues; children's question. Save. |
| 3–4 | Letter summary copy | If `1000-budget-assets/letter-summary-copy.md` exists: read, edit. If not: draft 2–3 page summary from THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION (opening, condition, seam, ask, children's question, closing). Save. |

**Approval gate:** Send both documents to principal: "Copy for one-pager and letter summary ready for approval. No design or spend until you approve."

**If waiting on approval:** Start Hour 5 (one-line library); return to designer brief after copy is approved.

---

## Hour 5 — One-line library (Step 0.2)

| Task | Action |
|------|--------|
| Awareness library | If `one-line-library-awareness.md` exists: read, add or trim lines so it's 10–15 universal lines (no Iran / "demand the vote"). If not: create from one-line-library.md, keep only peace/seam/one subject/children's question. Save in campaign folder. |
| Send | Include in same batch to principal as copy, or send separately: "One-line library for awareness campaign ready for approval." |

**Approval gate:** Principal approves list (or approves copy + list together).

---

## Hours 6–7 — Designer brief (Step 0.3 prep)

| Hours | Task | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 6 | Brief document | If `1000-budget-assets/designer-brief-simple-condition.md` exists: read, add principal contact and timeline. If not: create brief (deliverables: one-pager PDF, letter summary PDF, 3–5 quote graphics; quote lines; style; file names). Attach or reference one-pager-copy and letter-summary-copy. |
| 7 | Finalize and send | Insert exact quote lines (from approved one-line library or brief). Send brief to principal: "Designer brief ready. Pending your approval and approval of copy before I post job or hire." |

**Approval gate:** Principal approves brief (and copy, if not yet approved). No hire or spend until both are approved.

**If waiting on approval:** Start Hour 8 (mail list); return to hiring when approved.

---

## Hours 8–12 — Mail list (Step 0.6)

| Hours | Task | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 8–9 | Research | List 50–75 religious leaders/institutions: denominational offices (e.g. USCCB, URJ, ISNA, NCC, denominational HQs), seminary or religious-studies deans, interfaith councils, 1–2 named clergy or scholars per tradition. Use web search; note name, title, institution, address, city, state, ZIP. |
| 10–11 | Fill CSV | Open `1000-budget-assets/mail-list-template.csv`. Add rows. Columns: Name, Title, Institution, Address, City, State, ZIP, Email, Tradition_Notes, DateSent, Notes. Aim 50–75 rows. Save. |
| 12 | Check and send | Spot-check addresses (e.g. one per tradition). Send list to principal: "Mail list draft: N names. Confirm mail vs email for leaders, and approve list before any send." |

**Approval gate:** Principal approves list and channel (physical mail vs email).

**If waiting:** Draft clergy outreach email (Hour 17) or social captions (Hour 18) early.

## Hour 13 — Handoff prep and logs

| Task | Action |
|------|--------|
| Designer handoff | One-page note or checklist: "When hiring designer: send designer-brief + one-pager-copy + letter-summary-copy; budget $250–350; deadline 7–10 days; deliverables list." Save in 1000-budget-assets or as comment in brief. |
| Spend log | Ensure spend-log.csv has header row and one note row (budget total). No spend yet. |
| Results log | Ensure results-log.md has section headers; leave values blank until Phase 1. |

---

## Hours 14–16 — Post job and hire (Step 0.3 execute)

| Hours | Task | Action |
|-------|------|--------|
| 14 | Post job | On Fiverr or Upwork: post project (Simple Condition: one-pager PDF, letter summary PDF, 3–5 quote graphics). Paste brief; attach or link copy. Budget $250–350. Deadline 7–10 days. |
| 14–15 | Shortlist | When proposals arrive: pick 1–2 (portfolio, price, turnaround). Send to principal: "Recommended freelancer: [name], $XXX, X days. Approve to hire." |
| 16 | Hire and send | After principal approves: hire chosen freelancer. Send designer brief + one-pager-copy + letter-summary-copy. Confirm delivery date. Log in spend-log: "Committed: Designer assets, $XXX, [freelancer name]." (Or log first payment if paid upfront.) |

**Approval gate:** Principal approves freelancer and spend before you pay or commit.

**If no approval in 20 hours:** Note "Pending: principal approval to hire designer." Use Hours 17–20 for mail-list refinement, clergy email draft, and status doc.

## If principal approval is slow

- **After Hour 4 or 5:** If copy/library not yet approved, use Hours 6–12 for mail list and designer brief; send everything in one batch for approval.
- **After Hour 12:** If list not yet approved, use 14–16 to post job only (no hire until copy + brief approved); use 17–20 for clergy draft, captions, and status.
- **Never spend or hire** without principal approval. Log "Pending approval: [item]" in status doc.

---

*Principal may adjust this schedule (e.g. skip mail, do email only; or prioritize list over designer). When in doubt, ask.*
