# One-Line Library — Simple Condition Public Awareness

Shareable lines for the **general awareness campaign** (no Iran or single-policy CTA). Use for social, quote graphics, and memes. Source: [THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION.md](../../../civilization-memory/essays/THE-SIMPLE-CONDITION.md). For Iran-specific lines see [one-line-library.md](one-line-library.md).

---

## Peace as condition

1. The door is open. We're the ones holding it shut.
2. Peace isn't the reward. Peace is the condition.
3. What if the door has been open this entire time?
4. Every day we keep killing is another day we keep the door closed.
5. The price is not sacrifice. The price is stopping.
6. The door has been open this entire time.

---

## Beauty and blade / the seam

7. Every tradition carries beauty and a blade in the same hand. Make the seam visible.
8. The seam is the line between beauty and blade. Make it visible so people can choose.
9. You do not have to abandon your faith to put down the blade. You have to see the seam.
10. Choose the seam. Beauty or blade — you don't have to abandon your faith to put down the weapon.
11. The sacred and the sword — your tradition has both. Choose.

---

## The children's question

12. Does anyone have to die for this to be true? When the answer is no, that's the beauty. Keep it.
13. When the answer is yes, that's the blade. It was added to the story.
14. Does anyone have to die for this to be true? — The question every tradition can teach.

---

## One subject, many tongues

15. One subject, many tongues. We don't need one creed to coordinate.
16. Say it in your own language. The subject is the same.
17. We don't need one language. We need one thing we all point to.

---

## Universal / invitational

18. Making the seam visible is the solution.
19. Identification of the seam is the solution. Not imposition — visibility.
20. The simple condition belongs to no one. It requires no institution.

---

## Usage notes

- **Campaign:** Public awareness only; no "demand the vote" or conflict-specific CTA in this list.
- **Quote graphics:** Prioritize 1, 7, 12, 15 for first batch of designed cards.
- **A/B test:** Track which lines get the most shares and link-clicks.
- **Localisation:** Translate top performers for multilingual distribution.
