# WAP consulting charter

**Territory:** `work-political-consulting` (umbrella for political consulting ops)  
**Not legal advice.** Entity formation (LLC, etc.) is operator/counsel.

---

## Mission

Deliver **bounded, AI-assisted** campaign support: briefs, opposition tracking, message drafts, content-queue discipline, and civ-mem-informed speech/policy **scaffolds**. The **companion** approves strategy, pricing, and anything that ships externally. No autonomous political action; no posting without human sign-off.

---

## Jurisdictions served (service lines)

| Line | Examples | Preconditions |
|------|----------|----------------|
| **Federal (US)** | House, Senate, PACs, national issue campaigns | US election-law awareness; FEC where applicable |
| **State (US)** | Legislature, statewide, state party adjacent | State ethics / registration per [compliance-checklist.md](compliance-checklist.md) |
| **Local (US)** | Mayor, council, school board, ballot measure | Local disclosure rules |
| **International** | Only where **compliance checklist** is completed for that **country + principal** | No engagement until signed scoping + counsel if needed |

---

## What we do

- Diagnostic memos, weekly brief scaffolds, opposition notes, talking points (human-approved before use).
- Structured content queue + source registry patterns.
- Optional civ-mem retrieval for historical framing ([civ-mem-draft-protocol.md](civ-mem-draft-protocol.md) — human-always-approves).

## What we do not do

- Autonomous social post (SMM or principal accounts) without human publish step.
- Merge campaign claims into the companion **Record** without RECURSION-GATE + approval.
- Commit to cross-border work without compliance clearance.
- Guarantee wins, media placement, or legal outcomes.

---

## Pricing rails

- **Bitcoin** preferred where agreed (receipts per [revenue-log.md](revenue-log.md)).
- **Fiat** acceptable (e.g. Fiverr) — rail secondary to bounded deliverable.
