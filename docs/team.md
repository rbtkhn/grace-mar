# Team Plan

## First 5 Hires

### 1. CTO / AI Lead

**Why first**: Core product is AI with behavioral fingerprinting, anti-cheating, LLM integration. This is the technical moat.

**Profile**:
- Strong LLM engineering (fine-tuning, prompt engineering, RAG)
- On-device ML experience (privacy requirements)
- Cryptographic systems (tamper-evident logs)
- Startup experience, can architect *and* code

**Compensation**: 5-10% equity

---

### 2. Full-Stack / Mobile Engineer

**Why**: Apps that work. iOS first (wealthy family demographic). Web for parents/evaluators.

**Profile**:
- iOS native (Swift) — critical for on-device ML, privacy
- React/Next.js for web
- Can move fast, ship MVPs
- Comfortable with ambiguity

**Division with CTO**:
- CTO: AI/ML core, architecture, anti-cheating
- This person: User-facing apps, integrations, polish

**Compensation**: 2-4% equity

---

### 3. Product Designer (UX/UI)

**Why critical**: Children must *want* to use this for 12 years. Parents must trust it.

**Profile**:
- Experience designing for children
- Both UX (flows, research) and UI (visual design)
- Prototyping skills
- Understands gamification without gimmicks

**Deliverables**:
- Child-facing teaching interface
- Parent dashboard
- Evaluator query experience

**Compensation**: 2-3% equity

---

### 4. Head of Learning / Education Lead

**Why essential**: This is an education product. Wrong learning model = product doesn't work.

**Profile**:
- Learning science, educational psychology, curriculum design
- Child development experience (ages 6-18)
- Worked at innovative school or edtech
- Credibility with education buyers

**Responsibilities**:
- Design teaching interaction model
- Structure knowledge domains
- Define what "depth" means
- Advise on age-appropriate UX

**Compensation**: 2-3% equity

---

### 5. Head of Growth / Sales

**Why now**: Need customers, not just product. Wealthy families don't download random apps.

**Profile**:
- Experience selling to affluent consumers OR education institutions
- Network in private schools, homeschool communities, family offices
- High-touch, consultative sales
- Ideally a parent

**Responsibilities**:
- Find first 50 pilot families
- Build school relationships
- Create referral loops
- Collect testimonials

**Compensation**: 2-3% equity + commission

---

## Team Structure

```
Founder/CEO
├── CTO / AI Lead
│   └── AI core, architecture, anti-cheating
├── Full-Stack/Mobile Engineer
│   └── Apps, integrations, user-facing product
├── Product Designer
│   └── UX/UI for kids, parents, evaluators
├── Head of Learning
│   └── Pedagogy, curriculum, credibility
└── Head of Growth
    └── First customers, sales, partnerships
```

## What You're Not Hiring Yet

| Role | Why Wait |
|------|----------|
| Backend/Infra | Modern tools reduce need; CTO + Full-stack cover |
| Data Scientist | CTO handles ML; hire when you have user data |
| Marketing | Growth covers early marketing; brand after PMF |
| Customer Success | Handle early users yourself; hire at 100+ families |
| HR/Ops | Use contractors; hire at 15+ people |
