# K-12 companion-self for Schools — Colorado target

**Purpose:** Target the K-12 pilot plan ([k12-schools-pilot-playbook.md](k12-schools-pilot-playbook.md)) specifically at **Colorado** schools and operators. **Commercial offer:** [Rocky Record service brief](rocky-record-service-brief.md). Not legal advice.

## Why Colorado first

| Factor | Relevance |
|--------|-----------|
| **Operator / product home** | Grace-Mar reference instance aligns with Colorado companion context; time zone and local pilots easier. |
| **Charter + choice density** | Strong charter and innovation-school ecosystem — faster procurement than large districts. |
| **Microschools / pods** | Overlap with primary wedge (homeschool/microschool) already in [business-prospectus.md](business-prospectus.md). |
| **CDE resources** | Colorado Department of Education publishes data privacy expectations for local education providers — align DPA and staff training language. |

**ICP (Colorado):** Colorado **charter schools**, **private schools**, **microschools**, **innovation schools**, and **AI-forward tutoring networks** based in CO. **Traditional districts** remain later wave after 1–2 CO reference pilots.

---

## Colorado compliance (counsel to confirm)

| Law / source | Notes |
|--------------|--------|
| **Student Data Transparency and Security Act (HB 16-1423)** | Applies to local education providers; transparency, security, parent access, vendor contracts, breach reporting. Pilot DPA should reference school obligations under this act. |
| **SB 24-041 (children’s online data, effective Oct 1, 2025)** | Stronger protections for minors’ data (assessments, retention, ads/sale/profiling, geolocation). Counsel should map pilot data flows to this statute if serving CO minors online. |
| **Colorado Privacy Act (CPA)** | Public K-12 schools are **exempt** as controllers; vendor posture still reviewed with counsel. |
| **FERPA / COPPA** | Unchanged; still baseline for any US pilot. |

**Reference (official / secondary):** [CDE Data Privacy and Security](https://www.cde.state.co.us/dataprivacyandsecurity) · Colorado bill texts for HB 16-1423 and SB 24-041.
