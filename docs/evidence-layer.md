# Evidence Layer

The **Evidence Layer** holds **raw source materials** the system or operators use before governance.

**Examples:**

- Uploaded files  
- Transcripts  
- Operator or guardian notes  
- External documents  
- Structured intake responses  
- Logs and observations  

---

## Rules

- Preserve **provenance** when possible (who, when, source).
- Do **not** treat raw evidence as **governed truth** or as the **Record**.
- Conflicting evidence should remain **visible as conflicting** until reviewed and resolved through a governed path.
- Evidence may be **staged** into [prepared context](prepared-context-layer.md); staging is **not** approval.

See [state-model.md](state-model.md) for the full three-layer doctrine.
