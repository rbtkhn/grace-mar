# THINK → IX promotion rules

**Purpose:** When capability intake may move from **THINK** to **SELF** IX-A / IX-B / IX-C. **WORK** and **THINK** remain separate; promotion to identity still uses **RECURSION-GATE** only.

## Gate discipline (non-negotiable)

- Echo **`CANDIDATE-XXXX`** and **one-line summary** before merge per [AGENTS.md](../../AGENTS.md) and operator-style.
- **Record merge** = companion approval + `process_approved_candidates.py` — never implied by a THINK claim alone.

## When THINK stays intake only

- One-off reading or discussion with **no** durable in-character need.
- Exploratory interest not yet stable across sessions.
- Capability claim still **emerging** or **exposed** with thin evidence.

## When THINK may become IX-A (knowledge)

- Companion **retains and uses** the fact **in-character** across sessions.
- Evidence recurs (multiple READ refs or strong ACT).
- Operator intends stable **SELF-KNOWLEDGE** — stage **one** crisp line, not a paragraph dump.

## When THINK may become IX-B (curiosity)

- **Durable fascination** with a domain or question type (not one book mention).
- Operator wants the Voice to **surface** the interest when queried.

## When THINK may become IX-C (personality / style)

- **Stable interpretive preference** or expressive pattern evidenced repeatedly (not a single aesthetic take).

## When nothing should be promoted

- Hot takes, spoilers, or chat that should not define identity.
- Duplicates of existing IX lines.
- Content that violates Lexile or prompt policy if mirrored to Voice — fix or abstain.

See [ix-promotion-examples.md](ix-promotion-examples.md).
