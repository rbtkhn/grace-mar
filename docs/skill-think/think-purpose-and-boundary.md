# THINK — purpose and boundary

## What THINK is

**THINK** is the **intake-and-capability** surface for what the fork is learning and can evidence about **cognitive moves**: comprehension, vocabulary, interests, reasoning patterns, topic familiarity. It lives in Record-bound **SKILLS** (`users/[id]/self-skills.md` index → `skill-think.md` or `self-skill-think.md` per instance).

THINK is **not**:

- **Identity (SELF / IX-A–C)** — stable “who she is” lines belong in `self.md` **after gate** when promoted.
- **WORK** — planning, campaigns, integration tasks live in `docs/skill-work/` and `work-*.md` under `users/[id]/`, not in THINK doctrine.
- **A substitute for EVIDENCE** — READ/ACT entries remain in `self-archive.md` (EVIDENCE); THINK **interprets** intake, it does not replace dated evidence rows.

## Promotion to SELF IX

**Selective, not automatic.** A THINK line does not become IX knowledge or curiosity until it passes **RECURSION-GATE** and companion approval when identity-facing promotion is intended. See [think-to-ix-promotion-rules.md](think-to-ix-promotion-rules.md).

## Two ingestion paths (provenance)

| Path | When | Gate? |
|------|------|--------|
| **Direct operator edit** | After shared reading / study per [we-read-think-self-pipeline.md](../we-read-think-self-pipeline.md) | No — operator edits `skill-think.md` (or instance file) directly per policy |
| **Staged THINK candidate** | Analyst/human-teacher flows that stage YAML for approval | Yes — approved blocks merge into THINK surface per gate workflow |

Both are valid; track **which path** applied when maintaining structured [think-claims.json](../../artifacts/skill-think/think-claims.json).
