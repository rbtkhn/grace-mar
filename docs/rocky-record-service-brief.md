# Rocky Record — service brief (Colorado K-12)

**Commercial name:** **Rocky Record**  
**Protocol:** Companion-self / Identity Fork (Grace-Mar reference implementation)  
**Audience:** Colorado **charters, microschools, private schools, innovation schools**, and CO-based tutoring networks — not a full-district RFP product on day one.

---

## One line

Portable **learner identity** for Colorado schools: human-approved **Record**, queryable **Voice** (from that Record only), and **exports** your organization can hand to the next teacher or tutor. We do **not** replace your LMS, Seesaw, or SIS.

---

## Tiers

| Tier | Term | Includes | Price logic (negotiable) |
|------|------|----------|---------------------------|
| **Pilot CO** | 90 days or 1 semester | Up to **50** student instances, hosted Voice (Telegram/WeChat), merge playbook, onboarding call(s), exit metrics, DPA package for CO LEPs | Fixed **$3.5K–$12K** by cohort size + support hours |
| **Annual CO** | 12 months | Everything in Pilot + named success contact, capped office hours, **quarterly handoff export** offer, roster refresh once/year | **~$8–15/student/mo** annualized or flat band by cap — finance + counsel finalize |

---

## Deliverables

1. **Per-student instance** — Structured Record (SELF, SKILLS, EVIDENCE), RECURSION-GATE, merge only after human approve.
2. **Voice** — Chat channel; answers within **documented** knowledge; abstention outside boundary.
3. **Merge policy** — Parent-only, educator-only, or dual — documented in SOW ([k12-pilot-agreement-template.md](k12-pilot-agreement-template.md)).
4. **Exports** — PRP, identity markdown, optional full fork — bulk per class ([k12-schools-mvp.md](k12-schools-mvp.md)).
5. **Colorado compliance pack** — Checklist + DPA placeholders for **HB 16-1423** and **SB 24-041** — [k12-compliance-checklist.md](k12-compliance-checklist.md), [k12-dpa-placeholders.md](k12-dpa-placeholders.md); **counsel signoff before first paid pilot**.
