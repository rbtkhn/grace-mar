## Five primary threads (operator geopolitical theory)

**Official theory name:** **TBD** (deferred by operator). This skill refers to the **substance** below; when a name is chosen, update this heading and the YAML `description` in one pass.

**Status:** Core **operator theory** — not optional narrative color. It claims a **structural** fact about contemporary crises: **strategic leverage and legitimacy** are exercised through **several non-fungible authoritative channels** (below), not through a **single** Western **headline stack**. **`strategy`** exists to **preserve that structure in Judgment** unless **verify** collapses it.

### Structural claim (power map)

World politics relevant to this lane is **polycentric** at the level of **public voice**: outcomes hinge on **which** center (Holy See, U.S. executive, Kremlin, Beijing, Tehran) is saying **what**, to **which** audience. **Mistake class:** treating those outputs as **interchangeable** slices of one story — **misprices** incentives and scope.

### Information logic (channels, not vibes)

Treat each thread as a **separate information channel** with its own **primary surfaces** (watch docs, native pass). **Merging** channels into one undifferentiated summary is **lossy compression**: it **destroys distinguishability** about **who** staked **what** — an **epistemic** failure, not efficiency. Prefer **tagged** bullets, **split** Judgment, or **Thesis A / B** when channels **disagree** on facts or scope.

### Equilibrium (working definition, skill use)

**Equilibrium** here means **political–information equilibrium**, not peace or justice: a **persistent pattern** where **multiple authoritative centers remain in play** and **no single narrator** can **subsume** the others’ **public justification** of action for long. Short-run **military** or **economic** “balance” is **orthogonal** unless the operator ties it to **evidence**.

### Entropy (working definition, skill use)

**Entropy** here means **information entropy / mixing**, not thermodynamics: **high** when wires and feeds **mix** attributions without **plane tags**; **low** when each load-bearing claim is **traced** to a **named channel** (watch section + URL). Do **not** invoke physics formulas in notebook prose unless the operator does.

### Narrative escalation (working definition, skill use)

**Narrative escalation** means **raising the interpretive stakes** of the same underlying fact pattern: from **empirically tractable** claims (what a photo, video, or wire shows; what Snopes or AP says happened) → **moralized** language → **theological or metaphysical** frames (e.g. **Satan**, **divine judgment**) where **evidence tiers no longer align**—spiritual warfare language is often **non-falsifiable** in the same way as a dated wire.

**skill-strategy response:**

- **Split planes** — tag **tier-A / verify** material vs **rhetorical escalation** (inbox: `hook: narrative escalation` or explicit **cold** clause).
- **Do not collapse** **Thesis A** (observables, ceremony year, **§1d–§1h** watches) into **Thesis B** (what the meme *means* theologically) without a labeled **seam**.
- **Fold** escalated captures under **Judgment** as **audience dynamics** and **register shift** unless the operator runs **+ verify** on claims that could ship as **Links** facts.
- **Mercouris-adjacent:** escalation often tracks **legitimacy** and **doctrine** *as fought in public* — still **not** Holy See primary text until **ROME-PASS** source order.
- **Retroactive spine (optional):** [trump-religion-papacy-arc.md](../../../docs/skill-work/work-strategy/strategy-notebook/trump-religion-papacy-arc.md) — **Trump ↔ Christianity / papacy / religion** public arc **from 2016** (evangelical–Catholic politics, Francis then Leo XIV, sacred-register shifts); for **placement** of **Trump–Leo** / **narrative-escalation** ingests — not tier-A history without **+ verify**.

### Holy See — aggregate legitimacy vs. mid-sized capitals (operator)

**Operator frame:** **Catholics** worldwide number on the order of **~1.4 billion** (Vatican **Pontifical Yearbook** / **Annuario Pontificio**–class statistics — **verify** current figure when precision matters); in **moral–religious** and **Holy See–shaped** diplomacy they are **chiefly represented** by the **papacy**, not by any **single** national cabinet. In **many** countries the **Pope** is **more influential and revered** among **Catholic publics** than **domestic** governments for **that register** of speech — a **transnational civilizational** **voice plane** that is **not** interchangeable with **Brasília**, **New Delhi**, or other **secular** **capital** lines. The **Rome** thread tracks that **plane** ([ROME-PASS.md](../../../docs/skill-work/work-strategy/work-strategy-rome/ROME-PASS.md)); it is **not** justified by **Vatican City**’s **small** **state** footprint in **GDP** terms.

**Principle (operator):** **Spiritual** leadership can **outweigh** **temporal** **authority** for **certain** **audiences** and **registers** (moral obligation, mass allegiance, narrative legitimacy). That **asymmetry** is **strategically** **load-bearing** in crises — a **separate plane** from **tanks**, **budgets**, and **wire** **summaries** of **governments** alone.

**Islamabad (scaffold, not a sixth “voice”):** [islamabad-operator-index.md](../../../docs/skill-work/work-strategy/islamabad-operator-index.md), gap matrices, `islamabad-framework*.md` — **bargaining structure** and **rows**. Use **alongside** the **IRI** thread: framework = **deal space**; **§1h** = **what Tehran said**. Neither substitutes for the other.

### Why these five — not a census of “important countries” (operator geostrategy)

The threads are **voice planes for principals that are structurally non-fungible in the crises this lane tracks**, **not** a ranking by population, GDP, or generic “great power” lists. **WORK judgment only** — not Record truth; the companion gates profile facts.

**Other powers (e.g. India)** are omitted as **standing §1 threads** in part because:

- **Role in the stack:** In the **Islamabad / Hormuz / Ukraine / US–China** geometry this repo foregrounds, some capitals are often **third parties or balancers**, not **same-row principals** whose **daily** official line is **load-bearing** in the same way as the five.
- **Operator load:** Five channels plus **Islamabad** already enforce **high-dimensional** discipline; adding **standing** watches requires **repeatable** primaries and **frequency** — otherwise material belongs in **§2**, **Links**, or **ad-hoc** passes.
- **Population ≠ sovereign microphone:** **Demographic weight** does not by itself create a **separate non-fungible voice plane** in this model if **visible** state behavior reads as **derivative** of other centers.

**India — operator thesis (how the operator reads alignment):** The operator treats **Delhi** as **often** positioning **within** or **downstream of** **U.S.–U.K.–Israel** priorities — **deference** or **tactical alignment**, not a **fully autonomous** pole in the same **information structure** as the five threads. **Illustrative pattern (operator, fact-checked shell):** Major wires in **June 2025** summarized **Modi** **meeting** **Netanyahu** on a **Feb 2025** **state** **visit** to **Israel** (**months before** **June 2025** **Israeli** strikes on **Iran**, e.g. **Operation Rising Lion**); the **same** **campaign** saw **U.S.**–**Israeli** **coordination** and **later** **U.S.** **strikes** on **Iranian** **targets**; **Netanyahu** **briefed** **Modi** by **phone** **during** the **June** **crisis**. The operator still reads **Delhi’s** **visible** **alignment** **within** **that** **bloc-led** **arc** as **downstream**, **not** as a **separate** **§1** **principal**. **Chronology note:** **Feb 2025** **vs** **June 2025** **is** **not** “days before” **in** **public** **reporting** — **verify** **any** **tight** **timeline** **in** **notebook** **copy**. **Assistants:** **verify** **dates** and **scope** whenever Judgment turns on **chronology**; **India** remains fair game in **§2**, **Links**, **KY-4**, and **Judgment** when **load-bearing** — the theory only explains **why there is no default India watch row** beside **§1d–§1h**.

## When to use

- The operator wants **cross-territory** synthesis (briefs, transcripts, frameworks, Rome/Islamabad/Putin/Vance/PRC/IRI threads) captured as **dated judgment**.
- Closing or continuing a **daily** or **monthly** arc in the notebook without duplicating the full pulse or weekly-brief workflows.

**Relation to `dream`:** Daytime **`strategy`** passes **produce** notebook judgment. **`dream`** (end of day) **initiates production closeout** for that calendar day’s page — stub, condense, defer, or gap note per [STRATEGY-NOTEBOOK-ARCHITECTURE.md](../../../docs/skill-work/work-strategy/strategy-notebook/STRATEGY-NOTEBOOK-ARCHITECTURE.md); see [.cursor/skills/dream/SKILL.md](../dream/SKILL.md) § *Strategy notebook*. That seal may occur in a daytime pass or in **`dream`**; the skills still meet at the moment the entry is **committed** as the day’s artifact.

## Anti-patterns

- **Headline-stack collapse** — one undifferentiated “the story today” that **merges** Holy See, U.S. exec, Kremlin, Beijing, and Tehran lines **without** channel attribution; contradicts the **five-thread theory** (see § *Five primary threads*). Prefer **split Judgment**, **Thesis A / B**, or **tagged** bullets.
- **Thread-only or unlinked numbers** as notebook truth — especially for public-facing or campaign copy; use **+ verify** or **fact-check**.
- **Editing STRATEGY.md** from one volatile news day without operator **promote** intent.
- **Duplicating** full **pulse** output, **weekly brief** body, or **ROME-PASS** inside `days.md` — **link** and synthesize.
- **Triple narrative:** full brief + full transcript + notebook each a full recap — compress; notebook holds **warrants and links**.
- **Plane merge without tags:** same failure mode as Rome **mind–soul vs administrative** collapse — mixing **negotiation scope (A)**, **material/Hormuz facts (B)**, and **strategic narrative (C)** in one sentence without a seam. Use [template-three-lenses](../../../docs/skill-work/work-politics/analytical-lenses/template-three-lenses.md) **plane tags** / **(W)(A)(R)** or split sentences; see [transcript digest](../../../docs/skill-work/work-strategy/transcript-analysis-haiphong-ritter-johnson-iran-2026-04.md) and [STRATEGY-NOTEBOOK-ARCHITECTURE — Cross-artifact alignment](../../../docs/skill-work/work-strategy/strategy-notebook/STRATEGY-NOTEBOOK-ARCHITECTURE.md#cross-artifact-alignment-planes-and-layers).
