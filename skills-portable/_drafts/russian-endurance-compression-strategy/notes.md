# Persian + Russian dual strategy stack (operator notes)

Two complementary portable skills that cover different posture regimes:

| Condition | Primary skill | Logic |
|-----------|--------------|-------|
| Growth, scaling, coalition | Persian (tolerance / parity / compression) | Optimize regime; integrate or deter; switch for efficiency. |
| Sustained pressure, attrition | Russian (marginal resilience / temporal compression / rupture-regeneration) | Endure, compress, reconstitute; survive first, optimize later. |
| Post-crisis recovery | Russian → Persian handoff | Russian regeneration stabilizes; Persian tolerance or parity resumes scaling. |
| Peer competition without resolution | Persian parity + Russian temporal compression | Sustained deterrence (Persian) with mobilization discipline (Russian) when drift sets in. |

**Activation:** Either skill can be invoked independently. When both are relevant, the operator picks which lens to apply first; the agent does not pre-develop both.

**Not a governance system.** These are strategy pass lenses — they inform judgment during `skill-strategy` work. They do not create KPIs, tracking dashboards, quarterly reviews, or maturity scores. The operator evaluates whether a lens was useful after applying it.
